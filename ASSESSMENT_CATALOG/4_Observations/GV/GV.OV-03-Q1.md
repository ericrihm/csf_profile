### Strengths

- KPIs tracked quarterly: TTD (9 hrs), TTI (14 hrs), TTR-CJC (6 days), TTR-C (12 days)
- KRIs defined for top five risks with automated threshold alerting in ServiceNow
- Metrics communicated to senior leadership through quarterly CISO dashboard

### Gaps

- KPI benchmarking against industry peers not formalized
- Some KRI thresholds set based on initial estimates, not validated against operational data
