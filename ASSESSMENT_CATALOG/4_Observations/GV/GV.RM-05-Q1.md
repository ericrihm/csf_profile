## Findings

### Strengths

- Quarterly Board presentations, monthly CEO reports, weekly CISO-CEO 1:1s established
- Cross-department communication channels defined (Slack, ServiceNow, security committee meetings)
- Critical finding escalation path documented and tested

### Gaps

- Internal audit function not yet fully integrated into cybersecurity risk communication loop
- No formal mechanism for non-security staff to report emerging cyber risks upward
