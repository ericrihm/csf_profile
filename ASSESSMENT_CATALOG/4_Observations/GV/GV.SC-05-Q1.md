## Testing Methods

| Method | Performed | Notes |
|--------|-----------|-------|
| Examine | Yes | Reviewed vendor contracts, security addenda, SLA definitions, SBOM requirements |
| Interview | Yes | Legal Counsel, GRC Manager, Procurement Director |

## Findings

### Strengths

- Standardized security addenda included in all vendor contracts with tiered requirements
- SLAs defined for vulnerability remediation, incident notification, and compliance reporting
- Contractual requirements for vulnerability disclosure and SBOM provision established
- Information sharing rules defined in contracts with TLP classifications

### Gaps

- 2 of 10 sampled contracts missing complete security addenda (pre-template contracts from 2024)
- SBOM requirements only enforced for vendors onboarded after Q3 2025
