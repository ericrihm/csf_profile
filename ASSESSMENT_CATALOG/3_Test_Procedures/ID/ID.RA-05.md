# ID.RA-05: Risk-Informed Response Prioritization Test Procedures

**CSF Subcategory:** ID.RA-05 - Threats, vulnerabilities, likelihoods, and impacts are used to understand inherent risk and inform risk response prioritization

---

## Test Procedures

1. **Review Risk Prioritization Process**
   - Obtain documentation of risk response prioritization methodology
   - Verify risk register entries are prioritized by risk score
   - Confirm prioritization considers likelihood and impact
   - Check that resource allocation decisions reference risk priorities

2. **Validate Threat Models**
   - Review threat models for critical applications
   - Verify threat models identify data flows and trust boundaries
   - Check that threat models are maintained after changes
   - Confirm threat model findings inform risk responses

3. **Assess Risk-Informed Decision Making**
   - Review examples of security investments driven by risk assessment
   - Verify budget allocation aligns with identified risk priorities
   - Check for documented risk-based justification for security projects
   - Confirm risk prioritization informs vulnerability remediation order

4. **Review Risk Response Tracking**
   - Verify risk responses have documented timelines
   - Check completion rates for planned risk responses
   - Assess tracking mechanisms for response implementation
   - Confirm overdue responses have escalation procedures

5. **Evaluate Risk Quantification**
   - Assess whether financial impact estimation is performed
   - Check for risk quantification methodology (e.g., FAIR)
   - Verify risk communication uses business-relevant metrics
   - Review risk reporting to leadership

## Evidence Requests

- [ ] Risk response prioritization methodology
- [ ] Threat model documentation for critical applications
- [ ] Security investment justification referencing risk assessment
- [ ] Risk response timeline and completion tracking
- [ ] Risk reporting to leadership examples
- [ ] Budget allocation aligned to risk priorities

## Notes

The connection between risk assessment outputs and actual resource allocation decisions is the key indicator of maturity. Look for concrete evidence that risk findings drive security spending priorities.
