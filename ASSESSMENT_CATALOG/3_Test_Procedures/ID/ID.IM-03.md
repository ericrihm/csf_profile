# ID.IM-03: Operational Process Improvement Test Procedures

**CSF Subcategory:** ID.IM-03 - Improvements are identified from execution of operational processes, procedures, and activities

---

## Test Procedures

1. **Review Policy Review Process**
   - Obtain policy review schedule and records
   - Verify annual policy review cycle is maintained
   - Confirm policy updates incorporate operational lessons
   - Check for policy review approval documentation

2. **Validate Operational Metrics**
   - Review vulnerability management operational metrics
   - Check incident response metrics and trend analysis
   - Verify metrics are used to identify improvement areas
   - Assess metric coverage across operational domains

3. **Assess Lessons Learned Process**
   - Review post-incident lessons learned documentation
   - Verify lessons learned are tracked to completion
   - Check for structured lessons learned methodology
   - Confirm lessons learned inform process updates

4. **Review Supplier Collaboration**
   - Check for supplier lessons learned sessions
   - Review collaborative improvement activities with vendors
   - Assess supplier performance metrics and feedback
   - Verify supplier feedback is incorporated into improvements

5. **Evaluate Continuous Improvement Program**
   - Assess whether a formal continuous improvement program exists
   - Review trend analysis of operational performance
   - Check for improvement initiative tracking and prioritization
   - Verify improvement outcomes are measured

## Evidence Requests

- [ ] Policy review schedule and records
- [ ] Operational metrics dashboards
- [ ] Post-incident lessons learned reports
- [ ] Supplier collaboration records
- [ ] Improvement initiative tracking
- [ ] Trend analysis reports

## Notes

Distinguish between ad hoc improvements and a structured continuous improvement program. The former is reactive; the latter drives systematic operational maturity.
