# ID.IM-04: Cybersecurity Plan Management Test Procedures

**CSF Subcategory:** ID.IM-04 - Incident response plans and other cybersecurity plans that affect operations are established, communicated, maintained, and improved

---

## Test Procedures

1. **Review Plan Inventory**
   - Obtain list of all cybersecurity plans (IR, BC, DR, VMP)
   - Verify each plan's scope, owner, and last review date
   - Confirm plans exist for all critical services
   - Check for supply chain incident scenarios in plans

2. **Validate Plan Content**
   - Review incident response plan for completeness
   - Verify contact and communication information is current
   - Check that common scenario playbooks are included
   - Confirm plan activation criteria are defined

3. **Assess Plan Communication**
   - Verify plans are distributed to responsible parties
   - Check that affected stakeholders have access to plans
   - Review communication of plan updates
   - Confirm plan awareness through training or acknowledgment

4. **Review Plan Maintenance**
   - Verify annual plan review schedule
   - Check which plans have been reviewed in the current cycle
   - Identify plans overdue for review
   - Confirm plan updates are version-controlled

5. **Evaluate Plan Testing**
   - Review plan testing schedule and history
   - Check for functional testing beyond tabletop exercises
   - Verify disaster recovery plan testing evidence
   - Assess whether test results inform plan improvements

## Evidence Requests

- [ ] Cybersecurity plan inventory
- [ ] Incident response plan (current version)
- [ ] Business continuity plan (current version)
- [ ] Vulnerability management plan
- [ ] Plan distribution and acknowledgment records
- [ ] Plan review and update history

## Notes

Plans that are not tested and maintained degrade in value rapidly. Focus on identifying which plans have been tested under realistic conditions and which have only been reviewed on paper.
