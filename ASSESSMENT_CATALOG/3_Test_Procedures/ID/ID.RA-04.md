# ID.RA-04: Impact and Likelihood Assessment Test Procedures

**CSF Subcategory:** ID.RA-04 - Potential impacts and likelihoods of threats exploiting vulnerabilities are identified and recorded

---

## Test Procedures

1. **Review Risk Assessment Methodology**
   - Obtain the risk assessment methodology documentation
   - Verify likelihood and impact rating scales are defined
   - Confirm scoring criteria are documented and consistent
   - Check that methodology includes quantitative elements

2. **Validate Risk Register Entries**
   - Sample 5 risk register entries
   - Verify each has documented likelihood and impact ratings
   - Confirm ratings are supported by evidence or analysis
   - Check that risk scenarios include threat-vulnerability pairings

3. **Assess Business Impact Analysis**
   - Review BIA completion status across systems
   - Verify critical systems have completed BIA
   - Check that BIA results inform risk ratings
   - Confirm business leaders participated in BIA

4. **Review Cascading Failure Analysis**
   - Identify systems with cascading failure analysis
   - Verify analysis covers dependency chains
   - Check for systems of systems impact assessment
   - Confirm analysis is limited to critical systems only

5. **Evaluate Risk Assessment Currency**
   - Verify risk assessment frequency (annual workshops)
   - Check for event-driven risk assessment triggers
   - Review emerging threat scenario evaluation process
   - Confirm risk ratings are updated following significant changes

## Evidence Requests

- [ ] Risk assessment methodology documentation
- [ ] Risk register with likelihood and impact ratings
- [ ] Business impact analysis records
- [ ] Cascading failure analysis documentation
- [ ] Annual risk assessment workshop minutes
- [ ] Risk rating update records

## Notes

Assess whether the annual risk assessment cadence is sufficient given the pace of threat landscape changes. Continuous risk assessment capabilities through automated tools would strengthen this area.
