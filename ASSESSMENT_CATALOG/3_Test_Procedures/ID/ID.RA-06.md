# ID.RA-06: Risk Response Management Test Procedures

**CSF Subcategory:** ID.RA-06 - Risk responses are chosen, prioritized, planned, tracked, and communicated

---

## Test Procedures

1. **Review Risk Response Framework**
   - Obtain documentation of risk response options (accept, mitigate, transfer, avoid)
   - Verify criteria for selecting risk response type
   - Confirm vulnerability management plan includes response criteria
   - Check that compensating controls are documented when applicable

2. **Validate Risk Response Documentation**
   - Sample 5 risk register entries with documented responses
   - Verify response rationale is documented for each
   - Confirm risk acceptance decisions include management approval
   - Check that mitigated risks have implementation evidence

3. **Assess POA&M Tracking**
   - Review POA&M items for completeness and currency
   - Identify overdue items and escalation status
   - Verify milestone tracking and progress updates
   - Confirm POA&M items are reviewed regularly

4. **Review Stakeholder Communication**
   - Verify risk response decisions are communicated to stakeholders
   - Check governance meeting minutes for risk discussions
   - Assess communication of priority risk responses
   - Confirm affected parties are notified of risk decisions

5. **Evaluate Response Effectiveness**
   - Check for post-implementation review of risk responses
   - Verify accepted risks are periodically re-evaluated
   - Assess whether risk responses achieve intended risk reduction
   - Review metrics for risk response program effectiveness

## Evidence Requests

- [ ] Risk response framework documentation
- [ ] Risk register with response decisions and rationale
- [ ] POA&M tracking report
- [ ] Risk acceptance approvals with management signatures
- [ ] Governance meeting minutes with risk discussions
- [ ] Compensating control documentation

## Notes

The 12 overdue POA&M items warrant focused attention. Determine root causes for overdue items and assess whether the tracking and escalation process is functioning effectively.
