# ID.RA-10: Critical Supplier Assessment Test Procedures

**CSF Subcategory:** ID.RA-10 - Critical suppliers are assessed prior to acquisition

---

## Test Procedures

1. **Review Third-Party Risk Management Program**
   - Obtain third-party risk management policy
   - Verify supplier assessment criteria and methodology
   - Confirm critical supplier classification criteria
   - Check for tiered assessment based on supplier criticality

2. **Validate Pre-Acquisition Assessments**
   - Sample 3 critical supplier assessments
   - Verify assessments were completed prior to contract signing
   - Confirm security questionnaire responses were reviewed
   - Check SOC 2 or equivalent report review evidence

3. **Assess Supplier Risk Scoring**
   - Review supplier risk rating methodology
   - Verify risk ratings are documented for critical suppliers
   - Check for risk-based conditions in supplier contracts
   - Confirm unacceptable risk findings result in remediation

4. **Review Ongoing Monitoring**
   - Verify annual reassessment cadence for critical suppliers
   - Check for continuous monitoring capabilities
   - Assess supplier security incident notification requirements
   - Review fourth-party (sub-processor) risk assessment

5. **Evaluate Program Coverage**
   - Compare assessed suppliers against full supplier inventory
   - Identify critical suppliers that have not been assessed
   - Check for suppliers that bypass the assessment process
   - Verify new supplier onboarding includes assessment

## Evidence Requests

- [ ] Third-party risk management policy
- [ ] Critical supplier register
- [ ] Sample supplier risk assessments (3)
- [ ] SOC 2 report review documentation
- [ ] Supplier security questionnaire template
- [ ] Ongoing monitoring records

## Notes

Focus on whether the assessment process scales to cover all critical suppliers and whether smaller or departmental suppliers are bypassing the assessment. Fourth-party risk is an emerging gap area.
