# RC.CO-03: Communicate Recovery Activities and Progress

**CSF Subcategory:** RC.CO-03 - Recovery activities and progress in restoring operational capabilities are communicated to designated internal and external stakeholders

---

## Test Procedures

1. **Review recovery communication procedures and channels**
   - Obtain documentation defining how recovery progress is communicated to stakeholders
   - Verify that communication channels (Slack, direct channels) are documented for different stakeholder groups
   - Confirm that communication procedures specify frequency, format, and content of recovery updates
   - Check that communication protocols address both internal teams and external stakeholders (customers, partners)

2. **Evaluate stakeholder identification and notification lists**
   - Review the stakeholder notification matrix for recovery communications
   - Verify that internal stakeholders (senior leadership, engineering, support) have defined communication requirements
   - Confirm that external stakeholders (customers of the continuous authentication service) have notification procedures
   - Check that supplier and partner communication requirements are documented per contractual obligations

3. **Assess recovery information security during communication**
   - Verify that recovery information is shared securely and on a need-to-know basis
   - Confirm that communication procedures prevent premature or unauthorized disclosure of incident details
   - Check that Slack channels used for recovery coordination have appropriate access controls
   - Evaluate whether sensitive technical details are filtered from external communications

4. **Test communication execution history**
   - Review communication records from the 2024 security incident recovery
   - Verify that stakeholders received timely updates during recovery operations
   - Confirm that communication cadence was maintained throughout the recovery period
   - Evaluate whether communication effectiveness was assessed in post-incident reviews

5. **Evaluate crisis communication coordination with suppliers**
   - Review procedures for coordinating recovery communication with critical suppliers (AWS, other SaaS providers)
   - Verify that contractual information-sharing requirements are reflected in communication procedures
   - Confirm that supplier status updates are incorporated into internal recovery communications

## Notes

This test procedure evaluates how Alma Security communicates recovery progress to internal and external stakeholders. Incident communication procedures are documented, and stakeholder communication during recovery uses Slack and direct channels. The 2024 security incidents provide evidence of actual recovery communication execution. Key focus areas include whether communication procedures are rehearsed (noted as not rehearsed), whether the Slack-based communication approach is formalized with access controls and templates, and whether external stakeholder communication (particularly customers of the continuous authentication SaaS) is structured. The trust-rebuilding initiatives following the 2024 incidents suggest that communication gaps may have been identified.
