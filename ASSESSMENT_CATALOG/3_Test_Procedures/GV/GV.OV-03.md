# GV.OV-03: Risk Management Performance Test Procedures

**CSF Subcategory:** GV.OV-03 - Organizational cybersecurity risk management performance is evaluated and reviewed for adjustments needed

---

## Test Procedures

1. **Review KPI measurement and reporting**
   - Request documented KPIs and their measurement methodology
   - Verify TTD (7hrs), TTI (11hrs), TTR-CJC (6 days), TTR-C (9 days) are formally tracked
   - Confirm KPI targets are defined and performance against targets is measured
   - Check reporting frequency and distribution

2. **Examine KRI (Key Risk Indicator) tracking**
   - Request documented KRIs and risk trend analysis
   - Verify KRIs are linked to the five documented risks (R1-R5)
   - Confirm thresholds are defined that trigger escalation or response

3. **Validate senior leadership communication**
   - Review how cybersecurity metrics are communicated to CEO and Board
   - Verify quarterly Board reporting includes performance trends
   - Confirm metrics drive resource and priority decisions at leadership level

4. **Assess adjustment process based on performance data**
   - Request examples of adjustments made based on KPI/KRI review
   - Verify feedback loop exists between performance measurement and operational changes
   - Check that underperforming areas receive documented remediation plans

## Evidence Requests

- [ ] KPI documentation with targets and actuals
- [ ] KRI documentation with thresholds
- [ ] Board reporting materials with cybersecurity metrics
- [ ] Evidence of performance-driven adjustments (meeting minutes, action items)

## Notes

Alma tracks strong operational KPIs. Assessment should verify that performance measurement extends beyond detection and response metrics to cover governance, risk management, and supply chain domains as well.
