# GV.OC-01: Organizational Mission Test Procedures

**CSF Subcategory:** GV.OC-01 - The organizational mission is understood and informs cybersecurity risk management

---

## Test Procedures

1. **Review mission alignment documentation**
   - Request Alma Security's mission and vision statements
   - Verify cybersecurity strategy references organizational mission
   - Confirm security goals (G1-G8) trace back to business objectives

2. **Interview CISO on mission-risk linkage**
   - Ask how cybersecurity risk decisions connect to Alma's continuous authentication mission
   - Verify awareness of business growth targets (10K customers, $36M ARR trajectory)
   - Confirm security strategy explicitly supports product trust requirements

3. **Examine strategic planning artifacts**
   - Review most recent annual security strategy document
   - Verify mission context is referenced in risk register preamble
   - Check Board reporting materials for mission-risk connection

4. **Cross-reference with non-security leadership**
   - Interview VP Engineering or Product lead on mission-security alignment
   - Confirm shared understanding of how security enables business objectives

## Evidence Requests

- [ ] Mission and vision statements
- [ ] Cybersecurity strategy document referencing organizational mission
- [ ] Board reporting materials showing mission-risk alignment
- [ ] Security goals (G1-G8) documentation with business traceability

## Notes

Alma's position as a continuous authentication SaaS company creates a unique dynamic where the product mission and security mission are tightly coupled. Assessment should verify this linkage is formalized, not just assumed.
