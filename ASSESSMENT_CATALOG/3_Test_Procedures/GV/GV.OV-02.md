# GV.OV-02: Strategy Coverage Review Test Procedures

**CSF Subcategory:** GV.OV-02 - The cybersecurity risk management strategy is reviewed and adjusted to ensure coverage of organizational requirements and risks

---

## Test Procedures

1. **Review audit findings integration**
   - Request recent audit findings (internal or SOC 2 readiness)
   - Verify findings are mapped to cybersecurity strategy adjustments
   - Confirm compliance gaps are addressed in strategy updates

2. **Examine personnel performance oversight**
   - Review performance evaluations for cybersecurity-related roles
   - Verify security responsibilities are included in role descriptions
   - Confirm Nadia Khan (D&R Lead) and Chris Magann (VM Lead) have defined security performance metrics

3. **Validate incident-driven strategy review**
   - Request evidence of strategy reviews triggered by security incidents
   - Verify post-incident lessons learned feed into strategy updates
   - Check that incident trends are analyzed for strategic implications

4. **Interview CISO on strategy coverage assessment**
   - Ask how completeness of the cybersecurity strategy is evaluated
   - Verify regular gap assessments against CSF 2.0 or other frameworks
   - Confirm strategy review cadence (at minimum annually and after significant events)

## Evidence Requests

- [ ] Audit findings with strategy adjustment mapping
- [ ] Personnel performance evaluations for security roles
- [ ] Post-incident strategy review documentation
- [ ] Strategy gap assessment against CSF 2.0

## Notes

Strategy coverage should be assessed holistically. Alma's progression toward SOC 2 Type II provides a natural forcing function for strategy review, but assessment should verify this extends beyond compliance-driven adjustments.
