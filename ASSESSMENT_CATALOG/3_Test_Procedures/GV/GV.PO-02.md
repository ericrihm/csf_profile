# GV.PO-02: Policy Review and Update Test Procedures

**CSF Subcategory:** GV.PO-02 - Policy for managing cybersecurity risks is reviewed, updated, communicated, and enforced to reflect changes in requirements, threats, technology, and organizational mission

---

## Test Procedures

1. **Review policy revision history**
   - Request revision history for all cybersecurity policies
   - Verify policies have been reviewed within the documented review cycle
   - Identify any policies that are overdue for review
   - Check that revision triggers include regulatory changes, incidents, and technology shifts

2. **Validate change-driven policy updates**
   - Request examples of policy updates triggered by environmental changes
   - Verify that SOC 2 readiness findings drove policy improvements
   - Check whether threat landscape changes (e.g., AI-driven attacks) prompted policy updates

3. **Examine policy enforcement mechanisms**
   - Review how policy compliance is monitored and enforced
   - Verify consequences for non-compliance are documented
   - Check for automated enforcement where applicable (e.g., access controls enforcing access policy)

4. **Interview CISO on policy lifecycle management**
   - Ask about the policy review and approval workflow
   - Verify ownership is assigned for each policy
   - Confirm timeline exists for addressing identified policy gaps

## Evidence Requests

- [ ] Policy revision history and change logs
- [ ] Examples of change-triggered policy updates
- [ ] Policy compliance monitoring evidence
- [ ] Policy ownership and review schedule

## Notes

GV.PO-02 specifically tests whether policies are living documents. Alma's inconsistent review cycles are a known gap. Assessment should document which policies are current and which are overdue.
