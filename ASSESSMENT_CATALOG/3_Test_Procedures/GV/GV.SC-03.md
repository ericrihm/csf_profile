# GV.SC-03: SCRM Integration Test Procedures

**CSF Subcategory:** GV.SC-03 - Cybersecurity supply chain risk management is integrated into cybersecurity and enterprise risk management, risk assessment, and improvement processes

---

## Test Procedures

1. **Review integration with enterprise risk management**
   - Verify supply chain risks (R-05) are included in enterprise risk register
   - Check for common risk taxonomy between C-SCRM and ERM
   - Confirm integrated control sets for cybersecurity and supply chain risk

2. **Validate risk assessment integration**
   - Review whether supplier risk assessments use the same methodology as internal risk assessments
   - Verify supply chain risk findings feed into the enterprise risk register
   - Check for escalation of material supply chain risks to senior management

3. **Examine improvement process integration**
   - Verify supply chain risk management is included in continuous improvement processes
   - Check for lessons learned from vendor assessments driving program improvements
   - Confirm supply chain risk trends are analyzed alongside other risk domains

4. **Interview CISO on C-SCRM integration approach**
   - Ask how supply chain risks are aggregated with other cybersecurity risks
   - Verify supply chain risks receive equal governance attention as internal risks
   - Confirm integration approach is documented and not ad hoc

## Evidence Requests

- [ ] Enterprise risk register showing supply chain risk entries
- [ ] Integrated risk assessment methodology documentation
- [ ] Continuous improvement documentation including C-SCRM
- [ ] Risk escalation records for supply chain issues

## Notes

R-05 (third-party vendor risk) is documented in the enterprise risk register, which demonstrates baseline integration. Assessment should verify the integration extends to assessment methodology, reporting, and improvement processes.
