# GV.RM-06: Risk Calculation Methodology Test Procedures

**CSF Subcategory:** GV.RM-06 - A standardized method for calculating, documenting, categorizing, and prioritizing cybersecurity risks is established and used

---

## Test Procedures

1. **Review risk assessment methodology**
   - Request documented risk assessment methodology (qualitative, quantitative, or hybrid)
   - Verify consistent rating scales for likelihood and impact
   - Confirm risk categorization taxonomy is defined
   - Check for risk prioritization criteria at enterprise level

2. **Validate risk register consistency**
   - Sample 5+ risks from the register and verify consistent application of methodology
   - Check that likelihood and impact ratings use the documented scale
   - Verify risk scores are calculated consistently across all entries

3. **Examine risk documentation templates**
   - Request risk register template or ServiceNow risk module configuration
   - Verify template captures: description, likelihood, impact, owner, treatment, timeline
   - Check that risk categories support aggregation and cross-domain comparison

4. **Interview risk management personnel on methodology application**
   - Ask how new risks are rated using the established methodology
   - Verify training on risk assessment methodology for security team members
   - Confirm methodology is applied consistently regardless of who identifies the risk

## Evidence Requests

- [ ] Risk assessment methodology document
- [ ] Risk register with consistent rating application
- [ ] Risk documentation template or ServiceNow configuration
- [ ] Risk categorization taxonomy

## Notes

Consistency in risk calculation enables meaningful trending and prioritization. Assessment should verify the methodology produces comparable results across different risk domains and assessors.
