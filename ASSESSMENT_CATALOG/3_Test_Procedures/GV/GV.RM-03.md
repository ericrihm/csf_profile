# GV.RM-03: Enterprise Risk Integration Test Procedures

**CSF Subcategory:** GV.RM-03 - Cybersecurity risk management activities and outcomes are included in enterprise risk management processes

---

## Test Procedures

1. **Review enterprise risk management framework**
   - Request enterprise risk management (ERM) documentation
   - Verify cybersecurity risks are included alongside financial, operational, and compliance risks
   - Confirm common risk taxonomy and rating methodology across domains

2. **Validate cybersecurity risk escalation to ERM**
   - Review risk register for evidence of cybersecurity risks in enterprise context
   - Verify escalation criteria are defined for elevating cybersecurity risks
   - Check that the five documented risks (R1-R5) appear in enterprise risk reporting

3. **Examine cross-functional risk governance**
   - Verify cybersecurity risk managers participate in enterprise risk discussions
   - Check for integrated risk committee or cross-functional risk review meetings
   - Confirm CISO reports to CEO with enterprise risk context

4. **Interview CISO on ERM integration**
   - Ask how cybersecurity risks are aggregated with other enterprise risks
   - Verify process for ensuring cybersecurity risks are not siloed
   - Confirm regular touchpoints between security team and enterprise risk function

## Evidence Requests

- [ ] Enterprise risk management framework or policy
- [ ] Enterprise risk register showing cybersecurity risks
- [ ] Cross-functional risk committee meeting minutes
- [ ] Risk escalation criteria documentation

## Notes

The CISO reports directly to the CEO, which supports strong ERM integration. Assessment should verify this reporting line translates into practical integration of cybersecurity risks into enterprise-level decision-making.
