# GV.SC-02: Supply Chain Roles and Responsibilities Test Procedures

**CSF Subcategory:** GV.SC-02 - Cybersecurity roles and responsibilities for suppliers, customers, and partners are established, communicated, understood, and enforced

---

## Test Procedures

1. **Review C-SCRM role assignments**
   - Request documentation of roles responsible for supply chain risk management
   - Verify named individuals are accountable for C-SCRM activities
   - Confirm RACI or responsibility matrix exists for supply chain cybersecurity

2. **Validate supplier and partner role communication**
   - Review contracts or agreements that define supplier cybersecurity responsibilities
   - Verify information sharing protocols are established with critical suppliers
   - Check that customer-facing security responsibilities are documented

3. **Examine internal C-SCRM role communication**
   - Verify C-SCRM responsibilities are communicated internally
   - Check that job descriptions for relevant roles include supply chain risk duties
   - Confirm performance goals include C-SCRM metrics where applicable

4. **Interview procurement and security personnel**
   - Ask about clarity of C-SCRM roles between procurement and security teams
   - Verify understanding of escalation paths for supplier risk issues
   - Confirm coordination mechanisms between internal C-SCRM stakeholders

## Evidence Requests

- [ ] C-SCRM RACI matrix or responsibility assignments
- [ ] Supplier contracts with cybersecurity clauses
- [ ] Internal communication of C-SCRM roles
- [ ] Job descriptions with C-SCRM responsibilities

## Notes

With ServiceNow managing vendor relationships, assessment should verify that C-SCRM roles and responsibilities extend beyond tool administration to active risk management accountability.
