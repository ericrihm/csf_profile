# GV.RM-02: Risk Appetite and Tolerance Test Procedures

**CSF Subcategory:** GV.RM-02 - Risk appetite and risk tolerance statements are established, communicated, and maintained

---

## Test Procedures

1. **Review risk appetite statement**
   - Request the documented risk appetite statement
   - Verify it distinguishes between risk appetite and risk tolerance
   - Confirm thresholds are expressed in measurable, business-relevant terms
   - Check approval by CISO, CEO, and CFO (Board ratification status)

2. **Validate risk tolerance communication**
   - Verify risk appetite is communicated to department heads
   - Check whether risk tolerance thresholds are embedded in operational procedures
   - Confirm non-security leadership can articulate organizational risk appetite

3. **Examine tolerance-driven decisions**
   - Request examples where risk tolerance thresholds triggered treatment decisions
   - Cross-reference High/High risks (R-01, R-03) against tolerance thresholds
   - Verify escalation procedures activate when tolerance is exceeded

4. **Interview CEO or CFO on risk appetite governance**
   - Ask how risk appetite was determined and who was involved
   - Verify executive awareness and agreement with stated tolerance levels
   - Confirm periodic review process for appetite refinement

## Evidence Requests

- [ ] Risk appetite and tolerance statement
- [ ] Evidence of communication to leadership
- [ ] Examples of tolerance-driven treatment decisions
- [ ] Approval records (CISO, CEO, CFO, Board)
