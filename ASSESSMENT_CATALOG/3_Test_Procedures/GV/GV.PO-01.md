# GV.PO-01: Cybersecurity Policy Test Procedures

**CSF Subcategory:** GV.PO-01 - Policy for managing cybersecurity risks is established based on organizational context, cybersecurity strategy, and priorities

---

## Test Procedures

1. **Review information security policy framework**
   - Request master information security policy and supporting policies
   - Verify policies cover access management, data classification, incident response, third-party risk
   - Confirm policies include scope, roles, enforcement mechanisms, and review dates
   - Check for senior management approval signatures

2. **Validate policy alignment with strategy**
   - Cross-reference policy statements with cybersecurity strategy objectives (G1-G8)
   - Verify policies reflect current risk appetite and tolerance statements
   - Confirm policies address the five documented risks (R1-R5) where applicable

3. **Examine policy communication and acknowledgment**
   - Request evidence of policy dissemination to all employees
   - Verify acknowledgment tracking (new hire and annual re-acknowledgment)
   - Check that policies are accessible (intranet, shared drive, or policy management tool)

4. **Interview non-security personnel on policy awareness**
   - Ask 2-3 employees outside security team about key policy requirements
   - Verify they can identify where to find policies
   - Confirm understanding of their responsibilities under the policies

## Evidence Requests

- [ ] Information security policy and sub-policies
- [ ] Policy approval records (signatures, dates)
- [ ] Employee acknowledgment tracking records
- [ ] Policy distribution or accessibility evidence

## Notes

Alma has policies in place but review cycles have been inconsistent. Assessment should pay particular attention to whether policies have current review dates and whether the review cadence is formalized.
