# GV.SC-07: Supplier Risk Assessment and Monitoring Test Procedures

**CSF Subcategory:** GV.SC-07 - The risks posed by a supplier, their products and services, and other third parties are understood, recorded, prioritized, assessed, responded to, and monitored over the lifecycle of the relationship

---

## Test Procedures

1. **Review ongoing supplier risk assessment program**
   - Request supplier risk assessment schedule and methodology
   - Verify assessment frequency is based on supplier criticality
   - Confirm assessments cover compliance with contractual cybersecurity requirements

2. **Validate continuous monitoring practices**
   - Review monitoring mechanisms for critical suppliers (security ratings, threat intelligence)
   - Verify process for detecting changes in supplier risk profiles
   - Check for automated monitoring where applicable (e.g., security scorecard services)

3. **Examine supplier risk response process**
   - Request evidence of risk findings communicated to suppliers
   - Verify remediation tracking for identified supplier risks
   - Check for escalation process when suppliers fail to meet requirements

4. **Validate business continuity planning for supplier disruptions**
   - Review contingency plans for critical supplier failures
   - Verify alternative supplier identification for critical services
   - Check that supplier disruption scenarios are included in continuity planning

## Evidence Requests

- [ ] Supplier risk assessment schedule and completed assessments
- [ ] Continuous monitoring evidence (security ratings, alerts)
- [ ] Supplier risk remediation tracking
- [ ] Business continuity plans for supplier disruptions

## Notes

With 60% of Tier 1 vendors assessed, Alma is building assessment coverage. Assessment should evaluate whether ongoing monitoring complements point-in-time assessments for already-evaluated suppliers.
