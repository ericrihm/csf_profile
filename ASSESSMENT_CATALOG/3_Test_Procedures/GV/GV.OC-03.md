# GV.OC-03: Legal and Regulatory Requirements Test Procedures

**CSF Subcategory:** GV.OC-03 - Legal, regulatory, and contractual requirements regarding cybersecurity - including privacy and civil liberties obligations - are understood and managed

---

## Test Procedures

1. **Review regulatory obligations inventory**
   - Request documented list of applicable laws, regulations, and standards
   - Verify coverage of SOC 2, California privacy laws (CCPA/CPRA), and any sector-specific requirements
   - Confirm contractual security obligations from enterprise customers are tracked

2. **Examine compliance tracking process**
   - Review how regulatory changes are monitored and communicated
   - Verify assigned ownership for each compliance domain
   - Check that legal counsel reviews cybersecurity obligations periodically

3. **Interview CISO on regulatory alignment**
   - Ask how cybersecurity strategy aligns with legal requirements
   - Verify awareness of privacy obligations given continuous authentication data handling
   - Confirm contractual security clauses are reviewed before customer agreements are signed

4. **Validate contractual obligation management**
   - Sample 3-5 enterprise customer contracts for security clauses
   - Verify security team has visibility into contractual commitments
   - Check for SLA tracking related to security obligations

## Evidence Requests

- [ ] Regulatory obligations inventory or compliance matrix
- [ ] Legal review records for cybersecurity requirements
- [ ] Sample enterprise customer contracts with security clauses
- [ ] CCPA/CPRA compliance documentation

## Notes

Alma handles biometric and authentication data, which carries heightened privacy obligations. Assessment must verify the organization tracks these obligations systematically given the sensitivity of continuous authentication data.
