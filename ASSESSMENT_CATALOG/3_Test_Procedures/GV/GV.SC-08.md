# GV.SC-08: Supplier Incident Planning Test Procedures

**CSF Subcategory:** GV.SC-08 - Relevant suppliers and other third parties are included in incident planning, response, and recovery activities

---

## Test Procedures

1. **Review supplier incident response integration**
   - Request incident response plan sections addressing third-party involvement
   - Verify roles and responsibilities for supplier-related incidents are documented
   - Confirm communication protocols for incident notification between Alma and suppliers

2. **Validate supplier participation in exercises**
   - Check whether critical suppliers are included in tabletop exercises or simulations
   - Review exercise participation records for supplier involvement
   - Verify lessons learned are shared with participating suppliers

3. **Examine crisis communication with suppliers**
   - Review defined communication methods and escalation paths for supplier incidents
   - Verify contact information is current for critical supplier security contacts
   - Check for pre-established communication templates for supplier-related incidents

4. **Interview Nadia Khan (D&R Lead) on supplier incident coordination**
   - Ask about supplier involvement in incident response procedures
   - Verify CrowdStrike MDR coordination is documented and tested
   - Confirm post-incident lessons learned include supplier perspectives

## Evidence Requests

- [ ] Incident response plan with supplier provisions
- [ ] Exercise records showing supplier participation
- [ ] Supplier incident communication protocols
- [ ] CrowdStrike MDR coordination documentation

## Notes

CrowdStrike MDR as a compensating control for R-01 (understaffing) makes supplier incident coordination particularly important. Assessment should verify this relationship is integrated into incident planning.
