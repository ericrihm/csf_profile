# GV.OV-01: Strategy Outcome Review Test Procedures

**CSF Subcategory:** GV.OV-01 - Cybersecurity risk management strategy outcomes are reviewed to inform and adjust strategy and direction

---

## Test Procedures

1. **Review Board reporting on strategy outcomes**
   - Request last two quarterly Board Audit and Risk Committee presentations
   - Verify cybersecurity KPIs (TTD, TTI, TTR-CJC, TTR-C) are reported
   - Confirm strategy adjustments are documented based on outcome data
   - Check for documented decisions driven by KPI trends

2. **Examine KPI dashboard and trend analysis**
   - Review Splunk/security operations KPI dashboard
   - Verify baseline-to-current trend data is maintained (TTD: 81hrs baseline to 7hrs current)
   - Confirm KPIs are reviewed at defined intervals (monthly at minimum)

3. **Interview CISO on strategy adjustment process**
   - Ask for examples of strategy adjustments made based on outcome review
   - Verify linkage between KPI performance and resource allocation decisions
   - Confirm process for escalating underperforming areas

4. **Validate that outcomes inform investment decisions**
   - Review budget allocation decisions tied to strategy outcomes
   - Verify detection capability investments were driven by TTD improvement goals
   - Check that innovation-enabling approach is balanced against risk management

## Evidence Requests

- [ ] Board Audit and Risk Committee presentations (last 2 quarters)
- [ ] Security KPI dashboard with trend data
- [ ] Meeting minutes showing strategy adjustment decisions
- [ ] Budget allocation documentation linked to security outcomes

## Notes

Alma has demonstrated strong KPI-driven governance with TTD improving from 81 hours to 7 hours. Assessment should verify this data-driven approach is formalized and consistently drives strategic decisions.
