# DE.CM-01 Ex3: Rogue Wireless Network Detection

**Subcategory:** DE.CM-01 — Networks and network services are monitored to find potentially adverse events

**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** AC-02, AU-12, CA-07, CM-03, SC-05, SC-07, SI-04

## Implementation Example

> Monitor facilities for unauthorized or rogue wireless networks

## Alma Security Implementation

The Redwood City office wireless infrastructure includes rogue AP detection capabilities through the enterprise wireless controller, which scans for unauthorized SSIDs and alerts the IT team when unknown access points are detected in the facility. Quarterly wireless security assessments validate that no rogue access points have been deployed. The information security policy prohibits personal hotspots and unauthorized wireless equipment in office spaces.
