# DE.AE-04 Ex2: Manual Impact and Scope Estimation

**Subcategory:** DE.AE-04 — The estimated impact and scope of adverse events are understood

**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** PM-09, PM-11, PM-18, PM-28, PM-30

## Implementation Example

> A person creates their own estimates of impact and scope

## Alma Security Implementation

The SOC Manager manually assesses impact and scope for incidents that span multiple detection platforms or involve business context not captured in automated tools. Manual estimation considers customer tenant exposure, continuous authentication service availability impact, regulatory notification thresholds, and reputational risk. Nadia Khan's team documents impact estimates in incident tickets and revises them as investigation reveals additional affected systems or data.
