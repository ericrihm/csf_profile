# DE.CM-02 Ex2: Physical Access Record Review

**Subcategory:** DE.CM-02 — The physical environment is monitored to find potentially adverse events

**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** CA-07, PE-03, PE-06, PE-20

## Implementation Example

> Review and monitor physical access records (e.g., from visitor registration, sign-in sheets)

## Alma Security Implementation

The Redwood City office maintains a visitor management system requiring government-issued ID verification, host employee escort assignment, and visitor badge issuance with restricted access zones. Visitor logs are reviewed weekly by the facilities team and monthly by the security team to identify patterns such as excessive visits from single vendors or unauthorized zone access attempts. Visitor badges are time-limited and automatically deactivated at end of business day.
