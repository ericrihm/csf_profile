# DE.CM-02 Ex3: Physical Access Control Tamper Monitoring

**Subcategory:** DE.CM-02 — The physical environment is monitored to find potentially adverse events

**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** CA-07, PE-03, PE-06, PE-20

## Implementation Example

> Monitor physical access controls (e.g., locks, latches, hinge pins, alarms) for signs of tampering

## Alma Security Implementation

Server room and network closet access points at the Redwood City office are equipped with tamper-detection sensors on door frames and locking mechanisms that generate alerts to the facilities management system upon physical interference. Monthly physical security walkthroughs verify the integrity of locks, hinge pins, and cable runs in sensitive areas. The facilities team documents tamper checks and reports anomalies to the security team for investigation.
