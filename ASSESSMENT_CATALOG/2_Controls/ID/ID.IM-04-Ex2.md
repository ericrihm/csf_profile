## Alma Security Implementation

Alma Security maintains an incident response plan and a vulnerability management plan. Business continuity and disaster recovery plans are documented for critical services. Plans are communicated to responsible parties. Annual plan reviews are scheduled but not all plans have been reviewed in the current cycle.
