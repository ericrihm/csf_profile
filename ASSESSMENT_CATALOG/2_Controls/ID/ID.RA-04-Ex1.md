## Alma Security Implementation

Alma Security conducts risk assessments that evaluate threat likelihood and potential business impact. The risk register documents identified risk scenarios with likelihood and impact ratings. Business leaders participate in annual risk assessment workshops. Cascading failure analysis is performed for critical systems only.
