# RS.CO-03 Ex4: Senior Leadership Incident Status Updates

**Subcategory:** RS.CO-03 — Information is shared with designated internal and external stakeholders

**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** IR-04, IR-06, IR-07, SR-03, SR-08

## Implementation Example

> Regularly update senior leadership on the status of major incidents

## Alma Security Implementation

During major incidents, the CISO provides regular status updates to senior leadership through a defined escalation cadence: initial notification within 1 hour of declaration, status updates every 4 hours during active response, and daily summaries until resolution. Updates are communicated via secure executive briefing channel and include incident scope, business impact, response actions, and estimated resolution timeline. The SOC Manager prepares situation reports for CISO delivery.
