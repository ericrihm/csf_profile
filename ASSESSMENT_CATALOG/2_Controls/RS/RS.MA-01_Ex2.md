# RS.MA-01 Ex2: Third-Party Incident Response Assistance

**Subcategory:** RS.MA-01 — The incident response plan is executed in coordination with relevant third parties once an incident is declared

**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** IR-06, IR-07, IR-08, SR-03, SR-08

## Implementation Example

> Request incident response assistance from the organization's incident response outsourcer

## Alma Security Implementation

Alma Security maintains an incident response retainer with a third-party IR firm for situations exceeding internal D&R team capacity or requiring specialized forensic expertise. Engagement criteria and escalation procedures are documented in the incident response playbook, with the CISO authorized to activate the retainer. Contact information and engagement SLAs are reviewed quarterly to ensure currency and availability for critical incidents.
