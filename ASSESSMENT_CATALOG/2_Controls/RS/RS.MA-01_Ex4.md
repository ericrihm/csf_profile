# RS.MA-01 Ex4: Activation of Supporting Cybersecurity Plans

**Subcategory:** RS.MA-01 — The incident response plan is executed in coordination with relevant third parties once an incident is declared

**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** IR-06, IR-07, IR-08, SR-03, SR-08

## Implementation Example

> Initiate execution of additional cybersecurity plans as needed to support incident response (for example, business continuity and disaster recovery plans and the crisis communication plan)

## Alma Security Implementation

The incident response playbook includes decision criteria for activating supporting plans including business continuity, disaster recovery, and crisis communication. The incident lead evaluates whether BCDR activation is warranted based on incident scope, affected system criticality, and estimated recovery timeline. Legal and PR coordination activates the crisis communication plan for customer-impacting incidents, a procedure strengthened after the 2024 incidents that required coordinated external messaging.
