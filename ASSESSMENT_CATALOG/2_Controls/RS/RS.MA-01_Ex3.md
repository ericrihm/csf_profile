# RS.MA-01 Ex3: Incident Lead Designation

**Subcategory:** RS.MA-01 — The incident response plan is executed in coordination with relevant third parties once an incident is declared

**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** IR-06, IR-07, IR-08, SR-03, SR-08

## Implementation Example

> Designate an incident lead for each incident

## Alma Security Implementation

The SOC Manager designates an incident lead for each declared incident, documented in the ServiceNow SOC ticket. The incident lead is responsible for coordinating response activities, maintaining the incident timeline, directing analyst tasking, and serving as the single point of contact for status updates. For major incidents, Nadia Khan assumes the incident lead role directly, with SOC analysts supporting under her coordination.
