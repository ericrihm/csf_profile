# RS.MA-04 Ex2: Coordinated Incident Escalation

**Subcategory:** RS.MA-04 — Incidents are escalated or elevated as needed

**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** IR-04, IR-05, IR-06, IR-07

## Implementation Example

> Coordinate incident escalation or elevation with designated internal and external stakeholders

## Alma Security Implementation

Escalation criteria in the incident response playbook define thresholds for escalating incidents to the CISO, Legal, PR, and executive leadership based on severity escalation, scope expansion, or elapsed response time. Slack #security-alerts serves as the primary internal escalation channel with defined escalation chains reaching Nadia Khan, the CISO, and Legal/PR coordinators. External escalation to law enforcement, regulators, or third-party IR retainer follows documented criteria with CISO authorization.
