# RS.CO-02 Ex2: Business Partner and Customer Contractual Notification

**Subcategory:** RS.CO-02 — Internal and external stakeholders are notified of incidents

**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** IR-04, IR-06, IR-07, SR-03, SR-08

## Implementation Example

> Notify business partners and customers of incidents in accordance with contractual requirements

## Alma Security Implementation

Customer contracts include incident notification SLAs specifying timeframes and communication channels for security incidents affecting customer data. The D&R team triggers contractual notification workflows through ServiceNow when incidents meet customer-impact thresholds. Nadia Khan coordinates with the Customer Success team to ensure notifications reach appropriate customer security contacts. Notification records are maintained in ServiceNow for compliance documentation.
