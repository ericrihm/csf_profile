# RS.CO-03 Ex1: Secure Information Sharing per Response Plans

**Subcategory:** RS.CO-03 — Information is shared with designated internal and external stakeholders

**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** IR-04, IR-06, IR-07, SR-03, SR-08

## Implementation Example

> Securely share information consistent with response plans and information sharing agreements

## Alma Security Implementation

Incident information is shared through established secure channels defined in the incident response plan. Internal sharing uses Slack #security-alerts with role-based channel access. External sharing with partners and customers follows documented information sharing agreements that specify classification handling, transmission security, and authorized recipients. Sensitive incident details are encrypted in transit and shared only with pre-authorized contacts.
