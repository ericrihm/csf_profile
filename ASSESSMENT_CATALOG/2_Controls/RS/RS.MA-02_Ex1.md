# RS.MA-02 Ex1: Preliminary Incident Report Review

**Subcategory:** RS.MA-02 — Incident reports are triaged and validated

**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** IR-04, IR-05, IR-06

## Implementation Example

> Preliminarily review incident reports to confirm that they are cybersecurity-related and necessitate incident handling

## Alma Security Implementation

SOC analysts perform initial triage of all incoming security alerts and reported incidents to confirm cybersecurity relevance before committing response resources. Triage criteria documented in the incident response playbook distinguish between cybersecurity incidents, IT operational issues, and false positives. Triage decisions and rationale are recorded in ServiceNow SOC tickets. Dismissed reports are tracked for detection tuning and false positive rate reduction.
