# RS.MI-02 Ex3: Third-Party Eradication Actions

**Subcategory:** RS.MI-02 — Incidents are eradicated

**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** IR-04

## Implementation Example

> Allow a third party (e.g., managed security service provider) to perform eradication actions on the organization's behalf

## Alma Security Implementation

Alma Security's DFIR retainer agreement authorizes the external forensics provider to perform eradication actions during major incidents, including advanced malware removal, rootkit elimination, and compromised infrastructure rebuilds. The retainer defines scope boundaries and requires the third party to document all eradication actions in a format compatible with ServiceNow incident records. AWS Professional Services can assist with eradication of cloud-infrastructure-level compromises under the Enterprise Support agreement, coordinated through Nadia Khan as the incident lead.
