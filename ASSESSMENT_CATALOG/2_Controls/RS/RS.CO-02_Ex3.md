# RS.CO-02 Ex3: Law Enforcement and Regulatory Body Notification

**Subcategory:** RS.CO-02 — Internal and external stakeholders are notified of incidents

**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** IR-04, IR-06, IR-07, SR-03, SR-08

## Implementation Example

> Notify law enforcement agencies and regulatory bodies of incidents based on criteria in the incident response plan

## Alma Security Implementation

The incident response playbook defines criteria for law enforcement and regulatory notification based on incident type, severity, and data classification. Legal counsel determines notification obligations for each qualifying incident, coordinating with the CISO on timing and content. Contact information for FBI Cyber Division, local law enforcement, and relevant regulatory bodies is maintained in the playbook and reviewed quarterly for currency.
