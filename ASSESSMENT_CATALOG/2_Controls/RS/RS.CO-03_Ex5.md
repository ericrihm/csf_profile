# RS.CO-03 Ex5: Contractual Incident Information Sharing Protocols

**Subcategory:** RS.CO-03 — Information is shared with designated internal and external stakeholders

**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** IR-04, IR-06, IR-07, SR-03, SR-08

## Implementation Example

> Follow the rules and protocols defined in contracts for incident information sharing between the organization and its suppliers, customers, and partners

## Alma Security Implementation

Customer and partner contracts define incident information sharing obligations including notification timeframes, data classification handling, and permitted disclosure scope. The D&R team maintains a reference matrix mapping contractual sharing requirements by customer tier and partner type. ServiceNow workflows enforce contractual notification SLAs by triggering alerts when incident timelines approach contractual deadlines. Legal reviews outbound incident communications for contractual compliance before release.
