# RS.CO-03 Ex3: HR Notification for Malicious Insider Activity

**Subcategory:** RS.CO-03 — Information is shared with designated internal and external stakeholders

**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** IR-04, IR-06, IR-07, SR-03, SR-08

## Implementation Example

> Notify HR when malicious insider activity occurs

## Alma Security Implementation

The incident response playbook includes specific procedures for notifying HR when investigations reveal malicious insider activity. The SOC Manager escalates insider threat findings to the CISO, who coordinates with HR and Legal on appropriate response actions. Notification follows documented procedures to preserve investigation integrity while enabling HR to initiate personnel actions. Evidence handling for insider cases follows enhanced chain-of-custody requirements given potential legal proceedings.
