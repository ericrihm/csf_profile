# RS.CO-03 Ex6: Supplier Crisis Communication Coordination

**Subcategory:** RS.CO-03 — Information is shared with designated internal and external stakeholders

**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** IR-04, IR-06, IR-07, SR-03, SR-08

## Implementation Example

> Coordinate crisis communication methods between the organization and its critical suppliers

## Alma Security Implementation

Alma Security maintains documented crisis communication procedures with critical suppliers including cloud infrastructure providers, SentinelOne, and key SaaS dependencies. Communication channels, escalation contacts, and information sharing protocols are established in advance and tested during tabletop exercises. When incidents involve supply chain components, the D&R team activates supplier communication channels to coordinate investigation and containment across organizational boundaries.
