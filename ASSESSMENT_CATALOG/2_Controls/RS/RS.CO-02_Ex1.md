# RS.CO-02 Ex1: Breach Notification Procedures

**Subcategory:** RS.CO-02 — Internal and external stakeholders are notified of incidents

**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** IR-04, IR-06, IR-07, SR-03, SR-08

## Implementation Example

> Follow the organization's breach notification procedures after discovering a data breach incident, including notifying affected individuals

## Alma Security Implementation

Alma Security maintains documented breach notification procedures aligned with California CCPA, applicable state breach notification laws, and contractual obligations. Upon confirming a data breach, Legal coordinates with PR to execute notification within required timeframes. Customer notification templates are pre-approved by Legal and include breach description, affected data categories, remediation steps, and support contact information. The 2024 incident experience drove procedural improvements emphasizing speed and transparency.
