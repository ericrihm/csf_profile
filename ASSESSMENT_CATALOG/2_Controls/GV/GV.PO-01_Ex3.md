# GV.PO-01 Ex3: Require Senior Management Approval on Policy

**Subcategory:** GV.PO-01 — Policy for managing cybersecurity risks is established based on organizational context, cybersecurity strategy, and priorities
**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** AC-01, AT-01, AU-01, CA-01, CM-01, CP-01, IA-01, IR-01, MA-01, MP-01, PE-01, PL-01, PM-01, PS-01, PT-01, RA-01, SA-01, SC-01, SI-01, SR-01

## Implementation Example

> Require approval from senior management on policy.

## Alma Security Implementation

All cybersecurity policies require formal approval from the CISO and CEO before publication. The approval workflow is managed through ServiceNow with digital signatures, version control, and an audit trail. Board-level policies (e.g., risk appetite) additionally require Audit Committee sign-off during quarterly Board meetings.
