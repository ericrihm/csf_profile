# GV.PO-01 Ex4: Communicate Policy Across the Organization

**Subcategory:** GV.PO-01 — Policy for managing cybersecurity risks is established based on organizational context, cybersecurity strategy, and priorities
**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** AC-01, AT-01, AU-01, CA-01, CM-01, CP-01, IA-01, IR-01, MA-01, MP-01, PE-01, PL-01, PM-01, PS-01, PT-01, RA-01, SA-01, SC-01, SI-01, SR-01

## Implementation Example

> Communicate cybersecurity risk management policy and supporting processes and procedures across the organization.

## Alma Security Implementation

Cybersecurity policies are published on the internal wiki, announced via Slack during updates, and reinforced through quarterly security awareness town halls led by the CISO. New hires receive policy orientation during onboarding. Department heads are responsible for cascading policy changes to their teams, with completion tracked in ServiceNow.
