# GV.SC-09 Ex2: Provide Component Risk Reporting to Leaders

**Subcategory:** GV.SC-09 — Supply chain security practices are integrated into cybersecurity and enterprise risk management programs, and their performance is monitored throughout the technology product and service life cycle
**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** PM-09, PM-19, PM-28, PM-30, PM-31, RA-03, RA-07, SA-04, SA-09, SR-02, SR-03, SR-05, SR-06

## Implementation Example

> Periodically provide risk reporting to leaders about how acquired components are proven to be untampered and authentic.

## Alma Security Implementation

The CISO includes supply chain integrity metrics in quarterly Board reports: SBOM coverage for critical applications, integrity verification results for deployed software, and any supply chain anomalies detected. The security engineering team provides monthly reports to the CISO on component authenticity validation status. Exceptions and tampered component discoveries are escalated immediately to the CEO.
