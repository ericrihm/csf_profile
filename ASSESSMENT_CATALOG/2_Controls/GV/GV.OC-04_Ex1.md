# GV.OC-04 Ex1: Criticality Criteria for Services

**Subcategory:** GV.OC-04 — Critical objectives, capabilities, and services that external stakeholders depend on or expect from the organization are understood and communicated
**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** CP-02(08), PM-08, PM-11, PM-30(01), RA-09

## Implementation Example

> Establish criteria for determining the criticality of capabilities and services as viewed by internal and external stakeholders.

## Alma Security Implementation

Alma maintains a service criticality framework that rates capabilities based on customer impact, revenue dependency, and data sensitivity. The continuous authentication API is classified as Tier 1 critical. Criticality criteria are documented in the business impact analysis and reviewed annually by the CISO and VP Engineering.
