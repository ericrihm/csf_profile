# GV.RM-02 Ex3: Refine Risk Appetite Based on Risk Exposure

**Subcategory:** GV.RM-02 — Risk appetite and risk tolerance statements are established, communicated, and maintained
**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** PM-09

## Implementation Example

> Refine organizational objectives and risk appetite periodically based on known risk exposure and experience.

## Alma Security Implementation

The CISO reviews and refines risk appetite statements annually during strategic planning and after material incidents. The Q4 2025 refinement tightened third-party risk tolerance after a vendor security event. Risk exposure data from the risk register, incident history, and KPI trends inform adjustments, which the Board ratifies at the next quarterly meeting.
