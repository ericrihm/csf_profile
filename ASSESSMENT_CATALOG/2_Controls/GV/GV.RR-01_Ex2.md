# GV.RR-01 Ex2: Share Leaders' Expectations for Secure Culture

**Subcategory:** GV.RR-01 — Organizational leadership is responsible and accountable for cybersecurity risk and fosters a culture that is risk-aware, ethical, and continually improving
**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** PM-02, PM-19, PM-23, PM-24, PM-29

## Implementation Example

> Share leaders' expectations regarding a secure and ethical culture, especially when current events and trends may tempt some workers to act contrary to those expectations.

## Alma Security Implementation

The CEO communicates cybersecurity culture expectations in quarterly all-hands meetings and reinforces them during company-wide Slack messages following industry incidents. The CISO publishes monthly security awareness bulletins addressing current threats and ethical expectations. Leadership expectations for ethical behavior are codified in the Acceptable Use Policy and reinforced through annual training.
