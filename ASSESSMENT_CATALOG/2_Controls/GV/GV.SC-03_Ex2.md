# GV.SC-03 Ex2: Establish Integrated Control Sets

**Subcategory:** GV.SC-03 — Cybersecurity supply chain risk management is integrated into cybersecurity and enterprise risk management, risk assessment, and improvement processes
**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** AC-01, AT-01, AU-01, CA-01, CM-01, CP-01, IA-01, IR-01, MA-01, MP-01, PE-01, PL-01, PM-01, PM-09, PM-18, PM-30, PM-31, PS-01, PT-01, RA-01, RA-03, RA-07, SA-01, SC-01, SI-01, SR-01, SR-02, SR-03

## Implementation Example

> Establish integrated control sets for cybersecurity risk management and cybersecurity supply chain risk management.

## Alma Security Implementation

Alma Security has developed an integrated control framework where supply chain security controls are mapped alongside internal cybersecurity controls in ServiceNow. Vendor access controls are managed through the same IAM platform as employee access. Third-party risk assessments feed into the same risk register used for internal risks. The integrated approach enables consistent control evaluation and gap identification across internal and external risk domains.
