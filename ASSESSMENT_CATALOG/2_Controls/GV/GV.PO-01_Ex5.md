# GV.PO-01 Ex5: Require Personnel Policy Acknowledgment

**Subcategory:** GV.PO-01 — Policy for managing cybersecurity risks is established based on organizational context, cybersecurity strategy, and priorities
**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** AC-01, AT-01, AU-01, CA-01, CM-01, CP-01, IA-01, IR-01, MA-01, MP-01, PE-01, PL-01, PM-01, PS-01, PT-01, RA-01, SA-01, SC-01, SI-01, SR-01

## Implementation Example

> Require personnel to acknowledge receipt of policy when first hired, annually, and whenever policy is updated.

## Alma Security Implementation

All employees must digitally acknowledge the Information Security Policy and Acceptable Use Policy upon hire, annually during Q1, and within 14 days of any policy update. Acknowledgment is tracked through the HRIS system integrated with ServiceNow. Non-compliance triggers automated escalation to the employee's manager and HR after 7 days.
