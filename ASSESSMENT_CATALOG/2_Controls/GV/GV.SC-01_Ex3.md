# GV.SC-01 Ex3: Implement C-SCRM Program Processes

**Subcategory:** GV.SC-01 — A cybersecurity supply chain risk management program, strategy, objectives, policies, and processes are established and agreed to by organizational stakeholders
**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** PM-30, SR-02, SR-03

## Implementation Example

> Develop and implement program processes based on the strategy, objectives, policies, and procedures.

## Alma Security Implementation

Alma Security has implemented C-SCRM processes in ServiceNow covering the full vendor lifecycle: intake and risk classification, security questionnaire distribution, evidence review, risk scoring, ongoing monitoring, and contract renewal assessments. The GRC team operates these processes with defined SLAs (initial vendor assessment within 15 business days, annual reassessment for Tier 1 vendors). Process effectiveness is measured through coverage metrics and assessment completion rates.
