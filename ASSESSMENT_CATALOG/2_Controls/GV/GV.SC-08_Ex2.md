# GV.SC-08 Ex2: Document Supplier Incident Response Roles

**Subcategory:** GV.SC-08 — Relevant suppliers and other third parties are included in incident planning, response, and recovery activities
**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** CP-01, IR-01, SA-04, SA-09, SR-02, SR-03, SR-08

## Implementation Example

> Identify and document the roles and responsibilities of the organization and its suppliers for incident-related activities, such as incident response, recovery, and reporting.

## Alma Security Implementation

The incident response plan documents supplier-specific roles: Alma's SOC leads incident coordination, the vendor provides technical investigation support and remediation, legal manages breach notification requirements, and the GRC team assesses contract compliance. RACI matrices for supplier incidents are maintained in the IR playbook. Tier 1 vendors have designated incident contacts and escalation paths documented in ServiceNow.
