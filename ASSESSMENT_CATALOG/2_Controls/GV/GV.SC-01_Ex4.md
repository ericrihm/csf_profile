# GV.SC-01 Ex4: Cross-Organizational C-SCRM Alignment

**Subcategory:** GV.SC-01 — A cybersecurity supply chain risk management program, strategy, objectives, policies, and processes are established and agreed to by organizational stakeholders
**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** PM-30, SR-02, SR-03

## Implementation Example

> Establish a cross-organizational mechanism that ensures alignment between functions that contribute to cybersecurity supply chain risk management.

## Alma Security Implementation

Alma Security maintains a cross-functional C-SCRM committee with representatives from security, procurement, legal, engineering, and finance. The committee meets monthly to review new vendor onboarding decisions, assess supplier risk changes, and align on contractual security requirements. Procurement cannot approve new vendors without a completed security assessment, enforced through ServiceNow workflow gates.
