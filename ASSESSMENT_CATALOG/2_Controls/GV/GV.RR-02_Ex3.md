# GV.RR-02 Ex3: Include Cybersecurity in Personnel Descriptions

**Subcategory:** GV.RR-02 — Roles, responsibilities, and authorities related to cybersecurity risk management are established, communicated, understood, and enforced
**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** PM-02, PM-13, PM-19, PM-23, PM-24, PM-29

## Implementation Example

> Include cybersecurity responsibilities and performance requirements in personnel descriptions.

## Alma Security Implementation

All security team job descriptions include explicit cybersecurity responsibilities and performance metrics. Engineering roles include security requirements (secure coding practices, vulnerability remediation SLAs). Department heads have cybersecurity risk management responsibilities in their job descriptions. HR reviews all new job postings to ensure security responsibilities are included where applicable.
