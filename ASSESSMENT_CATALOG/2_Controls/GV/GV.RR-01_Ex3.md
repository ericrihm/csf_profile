# GV.RR-01 Ex3: CISO Maintains Comprehensive Risk Strategy

**Subcategory:** GV.RR-01 — Organizational leadership is responsible and accountable for cybersecurity risk and fosters a culture that is risk-aware, ethical, and continually improving
**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** PM-02, PM-19, PM-23, PM-24, PM-29

## Implementation Example

> Leaders direct the CISO to maintain a comprehensive cybersecurity risk strategy and review and update it at least annually and after major events.

## Alma Security Implementation

The CEO formally directs the CISO to maintain, review, and update the cybersecurity risk strategy. The strategy is reviewed annually during Q4 planning and updated after material incidents, significant business changes (funding, M&A), or major regulatory developments. The Board receives the updated strategy at each quarterly meeting, and the CEO provides written acknowledgment of strategy approval.
