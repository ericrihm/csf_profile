# GV.RM-02 Ex1: Determine and Communicate Risk Appetite

**Subcategory:** GV.RM-02 — Risk appetite and risk tolerance statements are established, communicated, and maintained
**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** PM-09

## Implementation Example

> Determine and communicate risk appetite statements that convey expectations about the appropriate level of risk for the organization.

## Alma Security Implementation

The CISO and CEO have established a formal risk appetite statement defining Alma's tolerance for cybersecurity risk across data categories (customer data: very low appetite; internal operational data: moderate appetite). The statement is approved by the Board, published on the internal wiki, and referenced in all risk assessment activities. It is communicated to department heads during quarterly security briefings.
