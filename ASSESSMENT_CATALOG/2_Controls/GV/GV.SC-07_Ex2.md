# GV.SC-07 Ex2: Evaluate Supplier Compliance Evidence

**Subcategory:** GV.SC-07 — The risks posed by a supplier, their products and services, and other third parties are understood, recorded, prioritized, assessed, responded to, and monitored over the course of the relationship
**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** RA-09, SA-04, SA-09, SR-03, SR-06

## Implementation Example

> Evaluate third parties' evidence of compliance with contractual cybersecurity requirements, such as self-attestations, certifications, and third-party audits.

## Alma Security Implementation

The GRC team evaluates vendor compliance evidence on the contractually agreed cadence: SOC 2 Type II reports are reviewed for control exceptions, penetration test reports are assessed for critical findings, and security questionnaire responses are validated against prior submissions. Evidence gaps or concerning findings are documented in ServiceNow and escalated to the vendor with remediation timelines. Persistent non-compliance triggers contract review with legal.
