# GV.RM-05 Ex2: Cross-Department Risk Communication

**Subcategory:** GV.RM-05 — Lines of communication across the organization are established for cybersecurity risks, including risks from suppliers and other third parties
**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** PM-09, PM-30

## Implementation Example

> Identify how all departments across the organization - such as management, operations, internal audit, legal, acquisition, and human resources - will communicate regarding cybersecurity risk.

## Alma Security Implementation

Alma Security maintains defined communication channels for cybersecurity risk across departments: the security team uses Slack channels for operational coordination, ServiceNow for formal risk tracking, and monthly cross-functional security committee meetings. Legal receives risk notifications for regulatory matters, HR is engaged for insider threat concerns, and procurement coordinates with the GRC team on vendor risk assessments.
