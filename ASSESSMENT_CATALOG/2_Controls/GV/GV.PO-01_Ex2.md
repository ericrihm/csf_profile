# GV.PO-01 Ex2: Periodically Review Policy and Processes

**Subcategory:** GV.PO-01 — Policy for managing cybersecurity risks is established based on organizational context, cybersecurity strategy, and priorities
**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** AC-01, AT-01, AU-01, CA-01, CM-01, CP-01, IA-01, IR-01, MA-01, MP-01, PE-01, PL-01, PM-01, PS-01, PT-01, RA-01, SA-01, SC-01, SI-01, SR-01

## Implementation Example

> Periodically review policy and supporting processes and procedures to ensure that they align with cybersecurity strategy objectives and priorities.

## Alma Security Implementation

Alma Security conducts annual policy reviews led by the GRC team, with off-cycle reviews triggered by major incidents, regulatory changes, or significant business shifts. The CISO signs off on all policy revisions, and the review cadence is tracked in ServiceNow with automated reminders 30 days before review deadlines.
