# GV.OV-02 Ex2: Personnel Performance Oversight

**Subcategory:** GV.OV-02 — The cybersecurity risk management strategy is reviewed and adjusted to ensure coverage of organizational requirements and risks
**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** PM-09, PM-19, PM-30, PM-31, RA-07, SR-06

## Implementation Example

> Review the performance oversight of those in cybersecurity-related roles to determine whether policy changes are needed.

## Alma Security Implementation

The CISO conducts semi-annual performance reviews for security team leads (Nadia Khan, Chris Magann, Gerry) with metrics tied to risk management objectives. Performance gaps are analyzed for potential policy or process improvements. Tigan Wang's VM Specialist role includes documented performance metrics for vulnerability remediation timelines.
