# GV.RM-06 Ex2: Create Risk Register Templates

**Subcategory:** GV.RM-06 — A standardized method for calculating, documenting, categorizing, and prioritizing cybersecurity risks is established and communicated
**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** PM-09, PM-18, PM-28, PM-30, RA-03

## Implementation Example

> Create and use templates (e.g., a risk register) to document cybersecurity risk information (e.g., risk description, exposure rating, treatment plan).

## Alma Security Implementation

Alma Security maintains a standardized risk register in ServiceNow GRC with fields for risk description, likelihood, impact, risk score, owner, treatment plan, target remediation date, and residual risk. The template is used consistently across all risk assessments, and the GRC team reviews the register monthly for completeness and accuracy. Risk entries include links to supporting evidence and control mappings.
