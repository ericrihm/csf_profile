# GV.OC-05 Ex2: Single Points of Failure

**Subcategory:** GV.OC-05 — Outcomes, capabilities, and services that the organization depends on are understood and communicated
**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** PM-11, PM-30, RA-07, SA-09, SR-05

## Implementation Example

> Identify and document external dependencies that are potential points of failure for the organization's critical services, and ensure this is communicated.

## Alma Security Implementation

Critical external dependencies are assessed for single-point-of-failure risk. AWS as the sole cloud provider is documented as a concentration risk with multi-region architecture as the primary mitigation. The CISO communicates dependency risks to the Board quarterly, and engineering maintains contingency documentation for critical service provider failures.
