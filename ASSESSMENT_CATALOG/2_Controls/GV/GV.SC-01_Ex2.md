# GV.SC-01 Ex2: Develop C-SCRM Program Plan

**Subcategory:** GV.SC-01 — A cybersecurity supply chain risk management program, strategy, objectives, policies, and processes are established and agreed to by organizational stakeholders
**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** PM-30, SR-02, SR-03

## Implementation Example

> Develop the cybersecurity supply chain risk management program, including a plan (with milestones for achieving objectives), policies, and procedures that guide implementation.

## Alma Security Implementation

The GRC team maintains a C-SCRM program plan with quarterly milestones for achieving vendor assessment coverage targets (currently 85% of Tier 1 vendors assessed). The plan includes policies for vendor onboarding, ongoing monitoring, and offboarding, with procedures documented in ServiceNow workflows. Program maturity is tracked against a 3-year roadmap with annual objectives reported to the CISO.
