# GV.SC-10 Ex5: Develop Supplier Transition Plans

**Subcategory:** GV.SC-10 — Cybersecurity supply chain risk management plans include provisions for activities that occur after the conclusion of a partnership or service agreement
**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** PM-31, RA-03, RA-05, RA-07, SA-04, SA-09, SR-02, SR-03, SR-05, SR-06

## Implementation Example

> Develop and execute a plan for terminating or transitioning supplier relationships that takes supply chain security risk into account.

## Alma Security Implementation

For Tier 1 vendor transitions, Alma Security develops detailed transition plans addressing security risks: parallel operations during migration, data integrity validation, access management during transition, and security testing of replacement solutions. Transition plans are reviewed by the CISO and include risk mitigation steps for the overlap period. The GRC team monitors transition progress through ServiceNow milestones and escalates delays that extend risk exposure.
