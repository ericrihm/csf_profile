# GV.RR-04 Ex1: Integrate Cybersecurity into HR Processes

**Subcategory:** GV.RR-04 — Cybersecurity is included in human resources practices
**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** PM-13, PS-01, PS-07, PS-09

## Implementation Example

> Integrate cybersecurity risk management considerations into human resources processes (e.g., personnel screening, onboarding, change notification, offboarding).

## Alma Security Implementation

HR processes include cybersecurity at every stage: background checks during screening, security policy acknowledgment and awareness training during onboarding, access review notifications when roles change, and systematic access revocation during offboarding. The HRIS system is integrated with ServiceNow for automated provisioning and deprovisioning workflows. The security team receives automated alerts for all personnel changes.
