# GV.RM-06 Ex4: Use Consistent Risk Categories

**Subcategory:** GV.RM-06 — A standardized method for calculating, documenting, categorizing, and prioritizing cybersecurity risks is established and communicated
**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** PM-09, PM-18, PM-28, PM-30, RA-03

## Implementation Example

> Use a consistent list of risk categories to support integrating, aggregating, and comparing cybersecurity risks.

## Alma Security Implementation

Alma Security uses a standardized risk taxonomy with categories aligned to NIST CSF functions: Governance, Asset, Vulnerability, Threat, Compliance, and Third-Party risks. Categories are configured in ServiceNow to enable aggregation and comparison across business units. The consistent taxonomy supports quarterly Board reporting, where risks are presented by category with trend analysis over time.
