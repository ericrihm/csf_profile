# GV.OV-03 Ex3: Senior Leadership Metrics Communication

**Subcategory:** GV.OV-03 — Organizational cybersecurity risk management performance is evaluated and reviewed for adjustments needed
**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** PM-04, PM-06, RA-07, SR-06

## Implementation Example

> Collect and communicate metrics on cybersecurity risk management with senior leadership.

## Alma Security Implementation

The CISO presents a quarterly cybersecurity metrics briefing to the CEO and Board Audit and Risk Committee. The briefing includes KPI trends, risk register status, treatment plan progress, and resource utilization. Metrics are formatted for executive consumption with trend analysis and business impact context. The CEO receives monthly interim updates on critical metrics.
