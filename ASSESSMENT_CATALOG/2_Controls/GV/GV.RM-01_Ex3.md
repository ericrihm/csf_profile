# GV.RM-01 Ex3: Senior Leaders Agree on Cybersecurity Objectives

**Subcategory:** GV.RM-01 — Risk management objectives are established and agreed to by organizational stakeholders
**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** PM-09, RA-07, SR-02

## Implementation Example

> Senior leaders agree about cybersecurity objectives and use them for measuring and managing risk and performance.

## Alma Security Implementation

The CEO, CFO, CTO, and CISO formally agree on annual cybersecurity objectives during Q4 strategic planning. Objectives are documented in the Board-approved cybersecurity strategy and referenced in quarterly executive reviews. The CISO reports performance against objectives in monthly leadership meetings, and deviations trigger escalation to the CEO within 48 hours.
