# GV.OC-02 Ex1: Internal Stakeholder Identification

**Subcategory:** GV.OC-02 — Internal and external stakeholders are understood, and their needs and expectations regarding cybersecurity risk management are determined
**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** PM-09, PM-18, PM-30, SR-03, SR-05, SR-06, SR-08

## Implementation Example

> Identify relevant internal stakeholders and their cybersecurity-related expectations (e.g., performance and risk expectations of officers, directors, and advisors; cultural expectations of employees).

## Alma Security Implementation

Alma maintains a stakeholder registry in ServiceNow identifying internal groups (engineering, product, sales, legal, HR, executive team) and their cybersecurity expectations. The CISO conducts semi-annual stakeholder needs assessments and incorporates findings into the security strategy. Internal expectations are tracked against security goals G1-G8.
