# GV.SC-04 Ex1: Develop Supplier Criticality Criteria

**Subcategory:** GV.SC-04 — Suppliers are known and prioritized by criticality
**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** RA-09, SA-09, SR-06

## Implementation Example

> Develop criteria for supplier criticality based on, for example, the sensitivity of data processed or held by a supplier, the degree of access to the organization's systems, and the importance of the supplier's product or service.

## Alma Security Implementation

Alma Security defines supplier criticality based on three factors: data sensitivity (access to customer PII, authentication data, or internal systems), system access level (production environment, development, or no access), and business dependency (mission-critical, important, or convenience). Criteria are documented in the Third Party Risk Management Policy and applied consistently through ServiceNow intake workflows during vendor onboarding.
