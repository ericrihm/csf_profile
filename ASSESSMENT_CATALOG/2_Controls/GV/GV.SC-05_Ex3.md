# GV.SC-05 Ex3: Define Information Sharing Rules with Suppliers

**Subcategory:** GV.SC-05 — Requirements to address cybersecurity risks in supply chains are established, prioritized, and integrated into contracts and other agreements with suppliers and other relevant third parties
**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** SA-04, SA-09, SR-03, SR-05, SR-06, SR-10

## Implementation Example

> Define the rules and protocols for information sharing between the organization and its suppliers, and address in agreements with those suppliers.

## Alma Security Implementation

Information sharing protocols with suppliers are defined in vendor agreements: NDAs govern all shared information, TLP classifications determine sharing scope, incident-related information exchange follows documented procedures, and threat intelligence sharing is limited to Tier 1 vendors with established trust. Protocols are documented in the Third Party Risk Management Policy and referenced in all vendor security addenda.
