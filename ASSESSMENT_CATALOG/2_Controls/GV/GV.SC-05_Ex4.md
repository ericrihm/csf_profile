# GV.SC-05 Ex4: Risk-Based Security Requirements in Agreements

**Subcategory:** GV.SC-05 — Requirements to address cybersecurity risks in supply chains are established, prioritized, and integrated into contracts and other agreements with suppliers and other relevant third parties
**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** SA-04, SA-09, SR-03, SR-05, SR-06, SR-10

## Implementation Example

> Manage risk by including security requirements in agreements based on their criticality and potential impact.

## Alma Security Implementation

Security requirements in vendor agreements are tiered based on criticality: Tier 1 vendors (customer data access) require SOC 2 Type II, penetration testing, and 24-hour incident notification; Tier 2 vendors require annual security questionnaires and 72-hour notification; Tier 3 vendors require standard security terms. Requirements are proportional to potential impact, documented in contract templates, and enforced through ServiceNow tracking.
