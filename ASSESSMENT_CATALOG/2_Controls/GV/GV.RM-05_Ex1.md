# GV.RM-05 Ex1: Update Senior Executives on Cybersecurity Posture

**Subcategory:** GV.RM-05 — Lines of communication across the organization are established for cybersecurity risks, including risks from suppliers and other third parties
**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** PM-09, PM-30

## Implementation Example

> Determine how to update senior executives, directors, and management on the organization's cybersecurity posture at agreed-upon intervals.

## Alma Security Implementation

The CISO provides quarterly Board presentations covering risk posture, KPI dashboards (TTD/TTI/TTR), incident summaries, and strategic initiative progress. Monthly written reports go to the CEO and CFO. The CISO has a standing weekly 1:1 with the CEO for real-time risk communication. Critical findings trigger immediate escalation via secure messaging channels.
