# GV.SC-08 Ex3: Include Suppliers in Incident Response Exercises

**Subcategory:** GV.SC-08 — Relevant suppliers and other third parties are included in incident planning, response, and recovery activities
**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** CP-01, IR-01, SA-04, SA-09, SR-02, SR-03, SR-08

## Implementation Example

> Include critical suppliers in incident response exercises and simulations.

## Alma Security Implementation

Alma Security invites Tier 1 vendors to participate in annual tabletop exercises simulating supply chain incident scenarios (vendor breach, service disruption, data compromise). Exercises test communication protocols, escalation procedures, and coordinated response capabilities. Lessons learned are shared with participating vendors and incorporated into both Alma's and the vendor's incident response procedures. Exercise results are documented and reported to the CISO.
