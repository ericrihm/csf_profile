# GV.SC-08 Ex5: Conduct Lessons Learned with Suppliers

**Subcategory:** GV.SC-08 — Relevant suppliers and other third parties are included in incident planning, response, and recovery activities
**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** CP-01, IR-01, SA-04, SA-09, SR-02, SR-03, SR-08

## Implementation Example

> Conduct collaborative lessons learned sessions with critical suppliers.

## Alma Security Implementation

After supplier-related incidents or exercises, Alma Security conducts joint lessons learned sessions with the affected vendor within 30 days. Sessions review what worked, what failed, and what improvements are needed on both sides. Action items are documented in ServiceNow with owners and deadlines. The GRC team tracks improvement implementation and incorporates findings into the C-SCRM program's continuous improvement process.
