# GV.OC-03 Ex2: Contractual Requirement Tracking

**Subcategory:** GV.OC-03 — Legal, regulatory, and contractual requirements regarding cybersecurity - including privacy and civil liberties obligations - are understood and managed
**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** AC-01, AT-01, AU-01, CA-01, CM-01, CP-01, IA-01, IR-01, MA-01, MP-01, PE-01, PL-01, PM-01, PM-28, PS-01, PT, PT-01, RA-01, SA-01, SC-01, SI-01, SR-01

## Implementation Example

> Determine a process to track and manage contractual requirements for cybersecurity management of the supply chain.

## Alma Security Implementation

ServiceNow tracks contractual cybersecurity requirements from enterprise customer agreements and supplier contracts. Legal reviews all new agreements for security clauses before execution. The procurement team maintains a clause library of standard cybersecurity contractual terms. Supply chain contractual obligations are reviewed as part of the quarterly vendor management cycle.
