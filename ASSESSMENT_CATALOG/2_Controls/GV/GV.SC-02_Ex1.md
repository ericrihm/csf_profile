# GV.SC-02 Ex1: Identify C-SCRM Responsible Roles

**Subcategory:** GV.SC-02 — Cybersecurity roles and responsibilities for suppliers, customers, and partners are established, communicated, understood, and enforced
**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** SR-02, SR-03, SR-05

## Implementation Example

> Identify one or more specific roles or positions that will be responsible and accountable for planning, resourcing, and executing cybersecurity supply chain risk management activities.

## Alma Security Implementation

The GRC Manager is the designated C-SCRM lead, responsible for day-to-day vendor risk management operations. The CISO holds accountability for the overall C-SCRM program. A dedicated Third-Party Risk Analyst on the GRC team executes vendor assessments and monitoring. Roles, responsibilities, and escalation paths are documented in the Third Party Risk Management Policy and RACI matrix in ServiceNow.
