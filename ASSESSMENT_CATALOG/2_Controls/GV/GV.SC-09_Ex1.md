# GV.SC-09 Ex1: Require Provenance Records for Technology

**Subcategory:** GV.SC-09 — Supply chain security practices are integrated into cybersecurity and enterprise risk management programs, and their performance is monitored throughout the technology product and service life cycle
**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** PM-09, PM-19, PM-28, PM-30, PM-31, RA-03, RA-07, SA-04, SA-09, SR-02, SR-03, SR-05, SR-06

## Implementation Example

> Policies and procedures require provenance records for all acquired technology products and services.

## Alma Security Implementation

Alma Security's procurement policy requires provenance documentation for all technology acquisitions: vendor origin verification, software supply chain attestations, and hardware chain-of-custody records for critical infrastructure. SBOMs serve as provenance records for software components. Provenance requirements are enforced through ServiceNow procurement workflows, and the security team validates provenance for Tier 1 vendor products before deployment.
