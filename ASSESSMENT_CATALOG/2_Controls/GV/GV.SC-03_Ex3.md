# GV.SC-03 Ex3: Integrate C-SCRM into Improvement Processes

**Subcategory:** GV.SC-03 — Cybersecurity supply chain risk management is integrated into cybersecurity and enterprise risk management, risk assessment, and improvement processes
**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** AC-01, AT-01, AU-01, CA-01, CM-01, CP-01, IA-01, IR-01, MA-01, MP-01, PE-01, PL-01, PM-01, PM-09, PM-18, PM-30, PM-31, PS-01, PT-01, RA-01, RA-03, RA-07, SA-01, SC-01, SI-01, SR-01, SR-02, SR-03

## Implementation Example

> Integrate cybersecurity supply chain risk management into improvement processes.

## Alma Security Implementation

Supply chain risk findings feed into Alma Security's continuous improvement process. Lessons learned from vendor incidents, assessment gaps, and near-misses are documented in ServiceNow and reviewed quarterly by the C-SCRM committee. Improvement actions are tracked as tickets with owners and deadlines. The GRC team measures program maturity annually against the C-SCRM roadmap and adjusts processes based on findings.
