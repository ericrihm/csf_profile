# GV.RR-02 Ex5: Articulate Responsibilities Across Functions

**Subcategory:** GV.RR-02 — Roles, responsibilities, and authorities related to cybersecurity risk management are established, communicated, understood, and enforced
**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** PM-02, PM-13, PM-19, PM-23, PM-24, PM-29

## Implementation Example

> Clearly articulate cybersecurity responsibilities within operations, risk functions, and internal audit.

## Alma Security Implementation

Cybersecurity responsibilities are explicitly defined for operations (infrastructure security, patching), risk functions (risk assessment, control monitoring), and internal audit (control testing, compliance validation). The CISO maintains a responsibility matrix published on the internal wiki that maps security activities to each functional area. Cross-functional handoff procedures are documented to prevent gaps between teams.
