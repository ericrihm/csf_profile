# GV.SC-10 Ex4: Verify Return or Disposal of Organization Data

**Subcategory:** GV.SC-10 — Cybersecurity supply chain risk management plans include provisions for activities that occur after the conclusion of a partnership or service agreement
**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** PM-31, RA-03, RA-05, RA-07, SA-04, SA-09, SR-02, SR-03, SR-05, SR-06

## Implementation Example

> Verify that assets containing the organization's data are returned or properly disposed of in a timely, controlled, and comprehensive manner.

## Alma Security Implementation

Vendor offboarding procedures require certification of data return or destruction within 30 days of contract termination. Vendors must provide written attestation of data destruction including methods used (e.g., cryptographic erasure, physical destruction). For Tier 1 vendors, the GRC team reviews destruction certificates and validates compliance. Contractual clauses specify data retention limits and destruction requirements enforced through the offboarding checklist in ServiceNow.
