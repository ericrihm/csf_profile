# GV.OV-02 Ex3: Incident-Driven Strategy Review

**Subcategory:** GV.OV-02 — The cybersecurity risk management strategy is reviewed and adjusted to ensure coverage of organizational requirements and risks
**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** PM-09, PM-19, PM-30, PM-31, RA-07, SR-06

## Implementation Example

> Review strategy in light of cybersecurity incidents.

## Alma Security Implementation

Post-incident reviews include explicit evaluation of whether the cybersecurity strategy adequately addressed the incident scenario. Nadia Khan (D&R Lead) documents strategy implications in post-incident reports. Material incidents trigger ad hoc strategy reviews with the CISO and affected business stakeholders within 14 days of incident closure.
