# GV.SC-04 Ex2: Maintain Prioritized Supplier Record

**Subcategory:** GV.SC-04 — Suppliers are known and prioritized by criticality
**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** RA-09, SA-09, SR-06

## Implementation Example

> Keep a record of all suppliers, and prioritize suppliers based on the criticality criteria.

## Alma Security Implementation

Alma Security maintains a comprehensive supplier inventory in ServiceNow with all active vendors categorized by criticality tier (Tier 1: mission-critical, Tier 2: important, Tier 3: standard). The inventory includes vendor name, services provided, data access level, contract dates, risk score, and last assessment date. The GRC team reviews and updates the inventory quarterly, and the "No PO No Pay" policy ensures all paid suppliers are captured in the system.
