# GV.RM-06 Ex3: Establish Risk Prioritization Criteria

**Subcategory:** GV.RM-06 — A standardized method for calculating, documenting, categorizing, and prioritizing cybersecurity risks is established and communicated
**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** PM-09, PM-18, PM-28, PM-30, RA-03

## Implementation Example

> Establish criteria for risk prioritization at the appropriate levels within the enterprise.

## Alma Security Implementation

Risk prioritization follows a tiered model: Critical risks (customer data exposure, platform availability) receive immediate CISO attention; High risks are addressed within 30 days; Medium risks within 90 days. Prioritization criteria weight business impact, data sensitivity, regulatory implications, and exploitability. The criteria are documented in the risk management framework and applied consistently through ServiceNow scoring algorithms.
