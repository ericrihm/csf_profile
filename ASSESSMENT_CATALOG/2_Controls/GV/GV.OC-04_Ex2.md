# GV.OC-04 Ex2: Business Impact Analysis

**Subcategory:** GV.OC-04 — Critical objectives, capabilities, and services that external stakeholders depend on or expect from the organization are understood and communicated
**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** CP-02(08), PM-08, PM-11, PM-30(01), RA-09

## Implementation Example

> Determine (e.g., from a business impact analysis) assets and business operations that are vital to achieving the mission and the potential impact of a loss or compromise of those assets or operations.

## Alma Security Implementation

A business impact analysis identifies the authentication platform, customer data stores, and API infrastructure as vital assets. Impact scenarios are quantified in terms of customer exposure, revenue loss, and reputational damage. The BIA informs disaster recovery priorities and resource allocation for security controls protecting critical assets.
