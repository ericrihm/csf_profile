# GV.SC-06 Ex2: Assess Supplier Cybersecurity Capabilities

**Subcategory:** GV.SC-06 — Planning and due diligence are performed to reduce risks before entering into formal supplier or other third-party relationships
**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** SA-04, SA-09, SR-05, SR-06

## Implementation Example

> Assess the suitability of the technology and cybersecurity capabilities and the risk management practices of prospective suppliers.

## Alma Security Implementation

The GRC team evaluates prospective suppliers' cybersecurity maturity by reviewing their security certifications, incident history, security architecture documentation, and risk management processes. Technical assessments include evaluating encryption standards, authentication mechanisms, and data handling practices. The evaluation results are documented in ServiceNow and factor into the procurement decision with a pass/fail recommendation from the CISO.
