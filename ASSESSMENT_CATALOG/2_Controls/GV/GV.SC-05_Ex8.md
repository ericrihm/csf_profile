# GV.SC-05 Ex8: Require Supplier Personnel Vetting

**Subcategory:** GV.SC-05 — Requirements to address cybersecurity risks in supply chains are established, prioritized, and integrated into contracts and other agreements with suppliers and other relevant third parties
**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** SA-04, SA-09, SR-03, SR-05, SR-06, SR-10

## Implementation Example

> Contractually require suppliers to vet their employees and guard against insider threats.

## Alma Security Implementation

Contracts with suppliers who have access to Alma's systems or customer data require background checks for assigned personnel, insider threat awareness programs, and notification of personnel changes affecting Alma's account. Tier 1 vendor contracts specify minimum screening requirements aligned with Alma's own personnel security standards. Compliance is verified through annual vendor questionnaires.
