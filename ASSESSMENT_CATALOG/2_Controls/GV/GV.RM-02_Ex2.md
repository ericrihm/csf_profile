# GV.RM-02 Ex2: Translate Risk Appetite into Tolerance Statements

**Subcategory:** GV.RM-02 — Risk appetite and risk tolerance statements are established, communicated, and maintained
**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** PM-09

## Implementation Example

> Translate risk appetite statements into specific, measurable, and broadly understandable risk tolerance statements.

## Alma Security Implementation

Alma Security translates its risk appetite into quantified tolerance thresholds: TTD must not exceed 24 hours, critical vulnerabilities must be patched within 72 hours, and third-party vendors with access to customer data must maintain SOC 2 certification. These tolerances are documented in the risk register and used as escalation triggers in ServiceNow when thresholds are breached.
