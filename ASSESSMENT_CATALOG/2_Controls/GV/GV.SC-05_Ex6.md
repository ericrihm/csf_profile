# GV.SC-05 Ex6: Require Supplier Vulnerability Disclosure

**Subcategory:** GV.SC-05 — Requirements to address cybersecurity risks in supply chains are established, prioritized, and integrated into contracts and other agreements with suppliers and other relevant third parties
**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** SA-04, SA-09, SR-03, SR-05, SR-06, SR-10

## Implementation Example

> Contractually require suppliers to disclose cybersecurity features, functions, and vulnerabilities of their products and services.

## Alma Security Implementation

Vendor contracts include clauses requiring disclosure of known vulnerabilities, security features, default configurations, and security-relevant product changes. Tier 1 vendors must provide vulnerability notifications within 48 hours of discovery and share patch timelines. The GRC team tracks disclosure compliance in ServiceNow and escalates non-compliance to the CISO for contract enforcement action.
