# GV.OC-01 Ex1: Share Organizational Mission

**Subcategory:** GV.OC-01 — The organizational mission is understood and informs cybersecurity risk management
**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** PM-11

## Implementation Example

> Share the organization's mission (e.g., through vision and mission statements, marketing, and service strategies) to provide a basis for identifying cybersecurity risks that may impede that mission.

## Alma Security Implementation

Alma Security's mission as a continuous authentication SaaS provider is published on the corporate website, investor materials, and internal wiki. The CISO references the mission statement in the cybersecurity strategy document, mapping each of the eight security goals (G1-G8) to specific business objectives. Quarterly Board presentations explicitly connect cybersecurity risk management decisions to mission enablement.
