# GV.OC-03 Ex1: Legal and Regulatory Tracking

**Subcategory:** GV.OC-03 — Legal, regulatory, and contractual requirements regarding cybersecurity - including privacy and civil liberties obligations - are understood and managed
**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** AC-01, AT-01, AU-01, CA-01, CM-01, CP-01, IA-01, IR-01, MA-01, MP-01, PE-01, PL-01, PM-01, PM-28, PS-01, PT, PT-01, RA-01, SA-01, SC-01, SI-01, SR-01

## Implementation Example

> Determine a process to track and manage legal and regulatory requirements regarding protection of individuals' privacy, civil rights, and civil liberties.

## Alma Security Implementation

Legal counsel and the CISO jointly maintain a regulatory obligations register tracking applicable requirements including CCPA/CPRA, SOC 2 criteria, and sector-specific data protection requirements. The register is reviewed quarterly and updated when new regulations are enacted or existing requirements change. Privacy obligations related to continuous authentication data (including biometric data handling) receive heightened tracking.
