# GV.RR-01 Ex1: Leaders Agree on Cybersecurity Roles

**Subcategory:** GV.RR-01 — Organizational leadership is responsible and accountable for cybersecurity risk and fosters a culture that is risk-aware, ethical, and continually improving
**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** PM-02, PM-19, PM-23, PM-24, PM-29

## Implementation Example

> Leaders (e.g., directors) agree on their roles and responsibilities in developing, implementing, and assessing the organization's cybersecurity strategy.

## Alma Security Implementation

The Board of Directors, CEO, and CISO have formally agreed on their respective cybersecurity roles: the Board provides oversight and approves risk appetite, the CEO holds ultimate accountability for risk management, and the CISO develops and executes the cybersecurity strategy. These roles are documented in the Board charter, the CISO job description, and the cybersecurity governance framework reviewed annually.
