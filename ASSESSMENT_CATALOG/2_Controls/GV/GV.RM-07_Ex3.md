# GV.RM-07 Ex3: Prioritize Positive Risks Alongside Negative Risks

**Subcategory:** GV.RM-07 — Strategic opportunities (i.e., positive risks) are characterized and are included in organizational cybersecurity risk discussions
**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** PM-09, PM-18, PM-28, PM-30, RA-03

## Implementation Example

> Calculate, document, and prioritize positive risks alongside negative risks.

## Alma Security Implementation

Alma Security's risk register includes both threat risks and opportunity risks, evaluated using the same scoring methodology. Positive risks (e.g., competitive advantage from advanced security posture, customer trust gains from transparency reporting) are prioritized by potential business value and feasibility. The quarterly Board risk report includes a dedicated section on strategic opportunities alongside the threat landscape.
