# GV.SC-03 Ex1: Identify C-SCRM and ERM Alignment Areas

**Subcategory:** GV.SC-03 — Cybersecurity supply chain risk management is integrated into cybersecurity and enterprise risk management, risk assessment, and improvement processes
**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** AC-01, AT-01, AU-01, CA-01, CM-01, CP-01, IA-01, IR-01, MA-01, MP-01, PE-01, PL-01, PM-01, PM-09, PM-18, PM-30, PM-31, PS-01, PT-01, RA-01, RA-03, RA-07, SA-01, SC-01, SI-01, SR-01, SR-02, SR-03

## Implementation Example

> Identify areas of alignment and overlap with cybersecurity and enterprise risk management.

## Alma Security Implementation

The CISO has mapped C-SCRM activities to the enterprise risk management framework, identifying alignment in risk assessment methodology, risk register integration, and Board reporting cadence. Supply chain risks are assessed using the same likelihood/impact matrix as enterprise risks and appear in the unified risk register in ServiceNow. The GRC team documents overlap areas to prevent duplication and ensure consistent risk treatment approaches.
