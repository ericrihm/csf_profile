# GV.SC-08 Ex4: Define Crisis Communication with Suppliers

**Subcategory:** GV.SC-08 — Relevant suppliers and other third parties are included in incident planning, response, and recovery activities
**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** CP-01, IR-01, SA-04, SA-09, SR-02, SR-03, SR-08

## Implementation Example

> Define and coordinate crisis communication methods and protocols between the organization and its critical suppliers.

## Alma Security Implementation

Alma Security establishes crisis communication channels with Tier 1 vendors during onboarding: primary and backup contacts, secure communication methods (encrypted email, dedicated Slack channels), escalation matrices, and communication frequency expectations during active incidents. Crisis communication protocols are tested during annual tabletop exercises and reviewed quarterly. The SOC maintains a vendor crisis contact directory updated every 90 days.
