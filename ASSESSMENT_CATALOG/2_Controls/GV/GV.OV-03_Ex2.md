# GV.OV-03 Ex2: KRI Review

**Subcategory:** GV.OV-03 — Organizational cybersecurity risk management performance is evaluated and reviewed for adjustments needed
**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** PM-04, PM-06, RA-07, SR-06

## Implementation Example

> Review key risk indicators (KRIs) to identify risks the organization faces, including likelihood and potential impact.

## Alma Security Implementation

KRIs are tracked for each of the five documented risks (R1-R5), with thresholds that trigger escalation. R-03 KRI (TTD trend) demonstrated effective risk management with improvement from 81-hour baseline to 7 hours. KRI reviews are conducted monthly by the CISO and inform the quarterly Board risk report. Threshold breaches trigger documented escalation actions.
