# GV.PO-02 Ex3: Update Policy for Legal and Regulatory Changes

**Subcategory:** GV.PO-02 — Policy for managing cybersecurity risks is reviewed, updated, communicated, and enforced to reflect changes in requirements, threats, technology, and organizational mission
**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** AC-01, AT-01, AU-01, CA-01, CM-01, CP-01, IA-01, IR-01, MA-01, MP-01, PE-01, PL-01, PM-01, PS-01, PT-01, RA-01, SA-01, SC-01, SI-01, SR-01

## Implementation Example

> Update policy to reflect changes in legal and regulatory requirements.

## Alma Security Implementation

The legal team monitors regulatory developments (SOC 2, GDPR, state privacy laws) and notifies the GRC team of changes requiring policy updates. A regulatory change tracker in ServiceNow maps each regulation to affected policies. The CISO and General Counsel jointly approve policy revisions driven by legal or regulatory changes within 60 days of the effective date.
