# GV.RM-04 Ex1: Specify Risk Acceptance and Avoidance Criteria

**Subcategory:** GV.RM-04 — Strategic direction that describes appropriate risk response options is established and communicated
**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** PM-09, PM-28, PM-30, SR-02

## Implementation Example

> Specify criteria for accepting and avoiding cybersecurity risk for various classifications of data and types of systems.

## Alma Security Implementation

Alma Security's risk response framework defines acceptance and avoidance criteria by data classification tier. Customer PII and authentication data require risk avoidance or transfer (cyber insurance); internal operational data allows risk acceptance with documented justification. Risk acceptance requires CISO approval for Medium risks and CEO approval for High risks, documented in ServiceNow with expiration dates and compensating controls.
