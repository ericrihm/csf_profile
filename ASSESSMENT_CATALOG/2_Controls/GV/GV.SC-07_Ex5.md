# GV.SC-07 Ex5: Plan for Supply Chain Interruptions

**Subcategory:** GV.SC-07 — The risks posed by a supplier, their products and services, and other third parties are understood, recorded, prioritized, assessed, responded to, and monitored over the course of the relationship
**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** RA-09, SA-04, SA-09, SR-03, SR-06

## Implementation Example

> Plan for unexpected supplier and supply chain-related interruptions to ensure business continuity.

## Alma Security Implementation

Alma Security maintains business continuity plans for Tier 1 vendor disruptions, including identified alternative suppliers, data portability procedures, and failover capabilities. The BCP team tests vendor disruption scenarios annually as part of tabletop exercises. For critical cloud services, multi-region deployment and vendor-agnostic architecture reduce single-vendor dependency. Contingency plans are documented in ServiceNow and reviewed quarterly.
