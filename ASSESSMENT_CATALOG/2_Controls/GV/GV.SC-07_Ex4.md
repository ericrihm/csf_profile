# GV.SC-07 Ex4: Monitor Supplier Risk Profile Changes

**Subcategory:** GV.SC-07 — The risks posed by a supplier, their products and services, and other third parties are understood, recorded, prioritized, assessed, responded to, and monitored over the course of the relationship
**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** RA-09, SA-04, SA-09, SR-03, SR-06

## Implementation Example

> Monitor critical suppliers, services, and products for changes to their risk profiles, and reevaluate risk profiles and risk treatment options accordingly.

## Alma Security Implementation

The GRC team monitors for risk profile changes through security rating feed alerts, vendor-reported incidents, news monitoring for supplier breaches, and M&A activity affecting suppliers. Material changes trigger reassessment: vendor acquisition prompts full re-evaluation, security incidents trigger immediate risk score review, and certification lapses trigger remediation tracking. Risk profile changes are documented in ServiceNow and reported to the CISO.
