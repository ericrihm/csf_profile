# GV.SC-05 Ex1: Specify Contractual Rights and Responsibilities

**Subcategory:** GV.SC-05 — Requirements to address cybersecurity risks in supply chains are established, prioritized, and integrated into contracts and other agreements with suppliers and other relevant third parties
**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** SA-04, SA-09, SR-03, SR-05, SR-06, SR-10

## Implementation Example

> Specify in contracts and other agreements the rights and responsibilities of the organization, the supplier, and any other relevant entities.

## Alma Security Implementation

Alma Security's vendor contracts include standardized security addenda specifying each party's rights and responsibilities: Alma's right to audit, the supplier's obligation to maintain security controls, incident notification requirements, data handling restrictions, and termination rights for security non-compliance. Legal and the GRC team jointly review all vendor contracts before execution. Template clauses are maintained in the contract management system.
