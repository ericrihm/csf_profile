# GV.RR-03 Ex1: Periodic Resource Adequacy Reviews

**Subcategory:** GV.RR-03 — Adequate resources are allocated commensurate with the cybersecurity risk strategy, roles, responsibilities, and policies
**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** PM-03

## Implementation Example

> Conduct periodic management reviews to ensure that those given cybersecurity risk management responsibilities have the necessary authority.

## Alma Security Implementation

The CEO conducts quarterly reviews with the CISO to assess whether the security team has adequate authority, staffing, and budget to execute the cybersecurity strategy. Reviews examine open headcount, budget utilization, and any authority gaps preventing effective risk management. The CISO reports resource constraints to the Board when they impact the ability to maintain acceptable risk levels.
