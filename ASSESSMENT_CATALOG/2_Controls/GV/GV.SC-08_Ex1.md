# GV.SC-08 Ex1: Establish Incident Reporting Rules with Suppliers

**Subcategory:** GV.SC-08 — Relevant suppliers and other third parties are included in incident planning, response, and recovery activities
**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** CP-01, IR-01, SA-04, SA-09, SR-02, SR-03, SR-08

## Implementation Example

> Define and use rules and protocols for reporting incident response and recovery activities and the status of those activities between the organization and its suppliers.

## Alma Security Implementation

Vendor contracts define incident reporting protocols: Tier 1 vendors must notify Alma within 24 hours of a confirmed incident, provide initial impact assessment within 48 hours, and deliver a full incident report within 30 days. Reporting templates and secure communication channels are established during onboarding. The SOC team maintains vendor incident contact lists and protocols in the incident response playbook.
