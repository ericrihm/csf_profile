# GV.SC-10 Ex1: Establish Relationship Termination Processes

**Subcategory:** GV.SC-10 — Cybersecurity supply chain risk management plans include provisions for activities that occur after the conclusion of a partnership or service agreement
**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** PM-31, RA-03, RA-05, RA-07, SA-04, SA-09, SR-02, SR-03, SR-05, SR-06

## Implementation Example

> Establish processes for terminating critical relationships under both normal and adverse circumstances.

## Alma Security Implementation

Alma Security maintains vendor offboarding procedures in ServiceNow for both planned and emergency terminations. Normal termination follows a 90-day transition plan with data migration, access revocation, and knowledge transfer. Adverse termination (security breach, contract violation) triggers immediate access revocation, data preservation for forensic purposes, and accelerated transition. The CISO approves termination plans for Tier 1 vendors.
