# GV.SC-07 Ex3: Monitor Critical Supplier Security Obligations

**Subcategory:** GV.SC-07 — The risks posed by a supplier, their products and services, and other third parties are understood, recorded, prioritized, assessed, responded to, and monitored over the course of the relationship
**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** RA-09, SA-04, SA-09, SR-03, SR-06

## Implementation Example

> Monitor critical suppliers to ensure that they are fulfilling their security obligations throughout the relationship using a variety of methods.

## Alma Security Implementation

Alma Security monitors Tier 1 vendors through continuous security rating services, annual SOC 2 report reviews, quarterly compliance check-ins, and automated alerts for publicly disclosed breaches. The GRC team maintains a monitoring dashboard in ServiceNow that aggregates vendor risk signals. Non-compliance with security obligations triggers a formal notification process with escalation to legal if unresolved within 30 days.
