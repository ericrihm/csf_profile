# GV.RM-07 Ex2: Identify and Document Stretch Goals

**Subcategory:** GV.RM-07 — Strategic opportunities (i.e., positive risks) are characterized and are included in organizational cybersecurity risk discussions
**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** PM-09, PM-18, PM-28, PM-30, RA-03

## Implementation Example

> Identify stretch goals and document them.

## Alma Security Implementation

The CISO defines annual stretch goals that push beyond baseline compliance: achieving ISO 27001 certification ahead of schedule, reducing TTD below industry benchmarks, and implementing automated compliance evidence collection. Stretch goals are documented in the cybersecurity strategy alongside required objectives and tracked in quarterly reviews with color-coded status indicators in the CISO dashboard.
