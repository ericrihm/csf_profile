# GV.RR-03 Ex2: Identify Resource Allocation Aligned to Risk Tolerance

**Subcategory:** GV.RR-03 — Adequate resources are allocated commensurate with the cybersecurity risk strategy, roles, responsibilities, and policies
**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** PM-03

## Implementation Example

> Identify resource allocation and investment in line with risk tolerance and response.

## Alma Security Implementation

The CISO and CFO jointly develop the annual cybersecurity budget, aligning resource allocation to documented risk tolerance levels and treatment plans. High-priority risks receive dedicated budget lines, and investment decisions are justified by risk reduction metrics. The 15-person security team budget includes headcount, tooling (ServiceNow, SIEM, EDR), training, and incident response retainer costs, all mapped to specific risk treatments.
