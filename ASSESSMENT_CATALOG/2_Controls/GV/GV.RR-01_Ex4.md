# GV.RR-01 Ex4: Review Authority and Coordination

**Subcategory:** GV.RR-01 — Organizational leadership is responsible and accountable for cybersecurity risk and fosters a culture that is risk-aware, ethical, and continually improving
**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** PM-02, PM-19, PM-23, PM-24, PM-29

## Implementation Example

> Conduct reviews to ensure adequate authority and coordination among those responsible for managing cybersecurity risk.

## Alma Security Implementation

The CEO conducts semi-annual reviews of the CISO's authority, budget, and cross-functional coordination effectiveness. The review assesses whether the CISO has adequate authority to enforce security controls, sufficient budget allocation, and effective coordination mechanisms with engineering, legal, and operations. Findings are documented and shared with the Board's Audit Committee.
