# GV.SC-02 Ex7: Communicate C-SCRM Roles Internally

**Subcategory:** GV.SC-02 — Cybersecurity roles and responsibilities for suppliers, customers, and partners are established, communicated, understood, and enforced
**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** SR-02, SR-03, SR-05

## Implementation Example

> Internally communicate cybersecurity supply chain risk management roles and responsibilities for third-party stakeholders.

## Alma Security Implementation

C-SCRM roles and responsibilities for third-party stakeholders are communicated through multiple channels: the Third Party Risk Management Policy on the internal wiki, onboarding materials for procurement staff, and quarterly C-SCRM committee briefings. Procurement, legal, and engineering teams understand their responsibilities in the vendor assessment process through role-specific training and ServiceNow workflow assignments.
