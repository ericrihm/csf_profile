# GV.RM-01 Ex2: Establish Measurable Risk Management Objectives

**Subcategory:** GV.RM-01 — Risk management objectives are established and agreed to by organizational stakeholders
**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** PM-09, RA-07, SR-02

## Implementation Example

> Establish measurable objectives for cybersecurity risk management (e.g., manage the quality of risk assessments through periodic review of the risk assessment approach).

## Alma Security Implementation

Alma Security defines measurable risk management objectives tied to KPIs tracked quarterly: TTD under 8 hours, TTI under 12 hours, TTR-Critical under 10 days. The GRC team conducts quarterly quality reviews of risk assessments to ensure consistency and completeness. Objectives are documented in the annual cybersecurity strategy and reported to the Board via the CISO's quarterly dashboard.
