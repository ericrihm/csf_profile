# GV.RR-02 Ex2: Document Accountability and Authority

**Subcategory:** GV.RR-02 — Roles, responsibilities, and authorities related to cybersecurity risk management are established, communicated, understood, and enforced
**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** PM-02, PM-13, PM-19, PM-23, PM-24, PM-29

## Implementation Example

> Document who is responsible and accountable for cybersecurity risk management activities and how those party will be held accountable.

## Alma Security Implementation

A RACI matrix in ServiceNow maps each cybersecurity function to responsible, accountable, consulted, and informed parties. The CISO is accountable for the overall program, with specific activities delegated to the 15-person security team. Accountability is enforced through quarterly performance reviews, documented in HR systems, and tied to individual OKRs for security team members.
