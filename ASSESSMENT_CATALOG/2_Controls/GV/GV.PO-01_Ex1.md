# GV.PO-01 Ex1: Create and Disseminate Risk Management Policy

**Subcategory:** GV.PO-01 — Policy for managing cybersecurity risks is established based on organizational context, cybersecurity strategy, and priorities
**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** AC-01, AT-01, AU-01, CA-01, CM-01, CP-01, IA-01, IR-01, MA-01, MP-01, PE-01, PL-01, PM-01, PS-01, PT-01, RA-01, SA-01, SC-01, SI-01, SR-01

## Implementation Example

> Create, disseminate, and maintain an understandable, usable risk management policy with statements of management intent, expectations, and direction.

## Alma Security Implementation

Alma Security maintains a comprehensive Information Security Policy approved by the CEO and CISO, published on the internal wiki and accessible to all 300 employees. The policy is written in plain language, includes management's risk appetite statements, and defines expectations for all personnel. Policy distribution is tracked through ServiceNow with acknowledgment receipts.
