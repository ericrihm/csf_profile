# GV.RM-04 Ex2: Determine Cybersecurity Insurance

**Subcategory:** GV.RM-04 — Strategic direction that describes appropriate risk response options is established and communicated
**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** PM-09, PM-28, PM-30, SR-02

## Implementation Example

> Determine whether to purchase cybersecurity insurance.

## Alma Security Implementation

Alma Security maintains a cyber liability insurance policy with $5M coverage, reviewed annually by the CFO and CISO. The policy covers data breach response costs, business interruption, and third-party liability. The insurance decision is documented in the enterprise risk register as a risk transfer mechanism, and coverage limits are validated against the organization's risk exposure profile during annual renewal.
