# GV.OV-02 Ex1: Audit Findings Review

**Subcategory:** GV.OV-02 — The cybersecurity risk management strategy is reviewed and adjusted to ensure coverage of organizational requirements and risks
**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** PM-09, PM-19, PM-30, PM-31, RA-07, SR-06

## Implementation Example

> Review audit findings to confirm whether the existing cybersecurity strategy has ensured compliance with requirements, including legal, regulatory, and contractual requirements.

## Alma Security Implementation

Internal audit and SOC 2 readiness findings are mapped to cybersecurity strategy gaps. The CISO reviews all audit findings within 30 days and updates the strategy to address identified compliance gaps. Findings are tracked in ServiceNow with remediation plans linked to strategy objectives and timeline commitments.
