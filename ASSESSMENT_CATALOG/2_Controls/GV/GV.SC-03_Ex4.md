# GV.SC-03 Ex4: Escalate Material Supply Chain Risks

**Subcategory:** GV.SC-03 — Cybersecurity supply chain risk management is integrated into cybersecurity and enterprise risk management, risk assessment, and improvement processes
**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** AC-01, AT-01, AU-01, CA-01, CM-01, CP-01, IA-01, IR-01, MA-01, MP-01, PE-01, PL-01, PM-01, PM-09, PM-18, PM-30, PM-31, PS-01, PT-01, RA-01, RA-03, RA-07, SA-01, SC-01, SI-01, SR-01, SR-02, SR-03

## Implementation Example

> Escalate material cybersecurity risks in supply chains to senior management, and address them at the same level as other types of risks with similar impact.

## Alma Security Implementation

Material supply chain risks (e.g., Tier 1 vendor breach, critical dependency compromise) are escalated to the CISO immediately and to the CEO within 24 hours. Supply chain risks are presented alongside internal cybersecurity and enterprise risks in quarterly Board reports using the same severity framework. The C-SCRM committee has authority to escalate vendor risks to the Enterprise Risk Committee when impact thresholds are exceeded.
