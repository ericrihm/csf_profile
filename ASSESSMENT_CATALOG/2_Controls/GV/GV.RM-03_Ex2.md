# GV.RM-03 Ex2: Include Cybersecurity in Enterprise Risk Planning

**Subcategory:** GV.RM-03 — Cybersecurity risk management activities and outcomes are included in enterprise risk management processes
**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** PM-03, PM-09, PM-30, RA-07, SR-02

## Implementation Example

> Include cybersecurity risk managers in enterprise risk management planning.

## Alma Security Implementation

The CISO participates in all enterprise risk management planning sessions alongside the CFO, General Counsel, and VP of Operations. Cybersecurity risks are presented alongside financial, operational, and compliance risks in a unified risk dashboard. The CISO has a standing seat on the Enterprise Risk Committee and contributes to annual enterprise risk assessment planning.
