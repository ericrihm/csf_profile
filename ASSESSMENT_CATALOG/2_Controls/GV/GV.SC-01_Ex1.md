# GV.SC-01 Ex1: Establish C-SCRM Strategy

**Subcategory:** GV.SC-01 — A cybersecurity supply chain risk management program, strategy, objectives, policies, and processes are established and agreed to by organizational stakeholders
**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** PM-30, SR-02, SR-03

## Implementation Example

> Establish a strategy that expresses the objectives of the cybersecurity supply chain risk management program.

## Alma Security Implementation

Alma Security's C-SCRM strategy defines objectives for managing third-party cybersecurity risk across the vendor lifecycle. The strategy prioritizes supplier visibility, risk-based tiering, contractual security requirements, and continuous monitoring. It is owned by the CISO, approved by the CEO, and reviewed annually. The strategy aligns with the enterprise risk management framework and is documented in the Third Party Risk Management Policy.
