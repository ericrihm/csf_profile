# GV.SC-10 Ex2: Plan for Component End-of-Life

**Subcategory:** GV.SC-10 — Cybersecurity supply chain risk management plans include provisions for activities that occur after the conclusion of a partnership or service agreement
**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** PM-31, RA-03, RA-05, RA-07, SA-04, SA-09, SR-02, SR-03, SR-05, SR-06

## Implementation Example

> Define and implement plans for component end-of-life maintenance support and obsolescence.

## Alma Security Implementation

The security engineering team maintains an end-of-life (EOL) tracker in ServiceNow that monitors vendor-announced EOL dates for all critical software and hardware components. EOL plans are created 12 months before support expiration, including migration alternatives, budget requirements, and transition timelines. The software and hardware inventories include EOL dates, and the CISO reviews at-risk components quarterly to prioritize migration efforts.
