# GV.SC-05 Ex2: Include Cybersecurity Requirements in Agreements

**Subcategory:** GV.SC-05 — Requirements to address cybersecurity risks in supply chains are established, prioritized, and integrated into contracts and other agreements with suppliers and other relevant third parties
**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** SA-04, SA-09, SR-03, SR-05, SR-06, SR-10

## Implementation Example

> Include all cybersecurity and supply chain requirements that third parties must follow and how compliance will be verified in agreements.

## Alma Security Implementation

Vendor agreements include specific cybersecurity requirements: encryption standards (AES-256 at rest, TLS 1.2+ in transit), access control requirements, vulnerability management SLAs, and incident response obligations. Compliance verification methods are specified per tier: Tier 1 vendors provide annual SOC 2 Type II reports, Tier 2 vendors complete security questionnaires annually, and all vendors agree to audit rights. Requirements are scaled based on criticality.
