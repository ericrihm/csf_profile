# GV.OC-02 Ex2: External Stakeholder Identification

**Subcategory:** GV.OC-02 — Internal and external stakeholders are understood, and their needs and expectations regarding cybersecurity risk management are determined
**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** PM-09, PM-18, PM-30, SR-03, SR-05, SR-06, SR-08

## Implementation Example

> Identify relevant external stakeholders and their cybersecurity-related expectations (e.g., privacy expectations of customers, security requirements of business partners, compliance expectations of regulators, ethics expectations of society).

## Alma Security Implementation

External stakeholders (enterprise customers, Series B investors, regulators, technology partners) are documented with cybersecurity expectations mapped to compliance and contractual requirements. Customer security questionnaire patterns inform the SOC 2 readiness roadmap. Investor security expectations from Series B due diligence are tracked as standing requirements.
