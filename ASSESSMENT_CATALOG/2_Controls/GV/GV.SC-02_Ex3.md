# GV.SC-02 Ex3: Create C-SCRM Responsibility Matrices

**Subcategory:** GV.SC-02 — Cybersecurity roles and responsibilities for suppliers, customers, and partners are established, communicated, understood, and enforced
**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** SR-02, SR-03, SR-05

## Implementation Example

> Create responsibility matrixes to document who will be responsible and accountable for cybersecurity supply chain risk management activities, and communicate them to those in the identified roles.

## Alma Security Implementation

A RACI matrix in ServiceNow maps C-SCRM activities (vendor intake, risk assessment, contract review, ongoing monitoring, offboarding) to responsible parties across security, procurement, legal, and engineering. The matrix is communicated to all assigned personnel during onboarding and reviewed during quarterly C-SCRM committee meetings. Updates are version-controlled and published on the internal wiki.
