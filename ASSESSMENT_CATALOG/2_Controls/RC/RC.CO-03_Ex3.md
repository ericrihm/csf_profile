# RC.CO-03_Ex3: Contractual Information Sharing Compliance

**Subcategory:** RC.CO-03 — Recovery activities and progress in restoring operational capabilities are communicated to designated internal and external stakeholders

**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** IR-04, IR-06, SR-08

## Implementation Example

> Ex3: Follow the rules and protocols defined in contracts for incident information sharing between the organization and its suppliers and other third parties

## Alma Security Implementation

Alma's customer contracts for the continuous authentication SaaS service include incident notification provisions, and the incident response playbook references customer notification requirements. Third-party vendor agreements with AWS include shared responsibility for incident communication. However, a consolidated register of contractual information-sharing obligations has not been compiled, and compliance with specific contractual notification timelines has not been independently verified.
