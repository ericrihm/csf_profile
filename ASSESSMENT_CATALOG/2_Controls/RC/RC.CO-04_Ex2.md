# RC.CO-04_Ex2: Recovery and Prevention Communication

**Subcategory:** RC.CO-04 — Public updates on incident recovery are shared using approved methods and messaging

**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** CP-02, IR-04

## Implementation Example

> Ex2: Explain the steps being taken to recover from the incident and to prevent a recurrence

## Alma Security Implementation

Alma communicated recovery steps and prevention measures to stakeholders during and after the 2024 security incidents as part of trust-rebuilding initiatives. Public communications described remediation actions taken and security improvements planned to prevent recurrence. The public trust score KPI tracks the effectiveness of these communications over time. A standardized template for explaining recovery steps and prevention measures to external audiences has not been formalized for future use.
