# RC.CO-04_Ex1: Breach Notification Procedures

**Subcategory:** RC.CO-04 — Public updates on incident recovery are shared using approved methods and messaging

**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** CP-02, IR-04

## Implementation Example

> Ex1: Follow the organization's breach notification procedures for recovering from a data breach incident, such as notifying affected individuals

## Alma Security Implementation

Alma's incident response playbook includes breach notification procedures that address regulatory requirements and affected individual notification. The 2024 security incidents triggered the breach notification process, with communications coordinated through legal review and leadership approval. Public trust score KPI tracking was established as part of the trust-rebuilding initiative following these incidents. Notification templates exist but have not been pre-approved for rapid deployment during future incidents.
