# RC.CO-03_Ex2: Senior Leadership Recovery Updates

**Subcategory:** RC.CO-03 — Recovery activities and progress in restoring operational capabilities are communicated to designated internal and external stakeholders

**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** IR-04, IR-06, SR-08

## Implementation Example

> Ex2: Regularly update senior leadership on recovery status and restoration progress for major incidents

## Alma Security Implementation

Senior leadership receives recovery status updates through Slack and direct channels during active incident recovery. The 2024 security incidents included leadership briefings on recovery progress and trust-rebuilding timelines. Communication cadence during recovery is ad hoc rather than defined at fixed intervals, and a formal leadership status report template for recovery events does not exist.
