# RC.CO-03_Ex4: Crisis Communication with Critical Suppliers

**Subcategory:** RC.CO-03 — Recovery activities and progress in restoring operational capabilities are communicated to designated internal and external stakeholders

**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** IR-04, IR-06, SR-08

## Implementation Example

> Ex4: Coordinate crisis communication between the organization and its critical suppliers

## Alma Security Implementation

Alma coordinates with AWS during infrastructure-related incidents through AWS Support channels and leverages AWS health dashboards for supplier-side status visibility. For incidents affecting the continuous authentication service, engineering communicates with relevant SaaS vendors through their respective support channels. Formal crisis communication procedures specifically addressing supplier coordination are not documented, and no joint crisis communication exercise has been conducted with critical suppliers.
