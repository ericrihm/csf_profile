# RC.RP-02_Ex2: Adaptive Recovery Replanning

**Subcategory:** RC.RP-02 — Recovery actions are selected, scoped, prioritized, and performed

**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** CP-10, IR-04, IR-08

## Implementation Example

> Ex2: Change planned recovery actions based on a reassessment of organizational needs and resources

## Alma Security Implementation

Alma's recovery approach allows real-time adjustment of recovery priorities through Slack-based coordination between engineering leadership and incident commanders. During the 2024 security incidents, recovery actions were adapted as the scope of impact became clearer. The DR plan under development through the Cloud Security Optimization project ($100K) will formalize criteria for reassessing and adjusting recovery actions based on evolving conditions and resource availability.
