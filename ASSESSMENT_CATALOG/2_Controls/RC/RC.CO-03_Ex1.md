# RC.CO-03_Ex1: Secure Recovery Information Sharing

**Subcategory:** RC.CO-03 — Recovery activities and progress in restoring operational capabilities are communicated to designated internal and external stakeholders

**NIST SP 800-53 Ref:** IR-04, IR-06, SR-08

## Implementation Example

> Ex1: Securely share recovery information, including restoration progress, consistent with response plans and information sharing agreements or other contractual agreements

## Alma Security Implementation

Alma shares recovery information through Slack channels and direct communication with designated stakeholders, following the communication procedures documented in the incident response playbook. Recovery status updates include restoration progress and estimated timelines. Information sharing is limited to authorized personnel, though formal information-sharing agreements with external partners are not documented and Slack channel access controls for recovery communications have not been formally reviewed.
